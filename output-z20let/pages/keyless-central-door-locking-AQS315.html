Keyless Central Door Locking

A keyless central door locking system is available as an option for the Speedster/VX220 Turbo.

Both doors may be locked and unlocked using the remote control unit located in the ignition key fob. There is also a control switch located on the steering column cowl.

The system includes a secondary battery, located in the engine compartment and charged by the vehicle electrical system. In the event of battery failure, access to the vehicle may be gained via an override button located at the rear of the vehicle, in the upper recess of the upper number plate.
