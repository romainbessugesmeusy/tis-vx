Tyre Puncture Emergency Tyre Inflator Aerosol  

An aerosol for the emergency inflation of tyres is stowed in the front compartment of the vehicle.  

The aerosol must not be stowed in the passenger compartment.

The use of the aerosol does not produce a permanent repair to the tyre, but only enables the vehicle to be driven at a speed of up to 44 km/h (30 mph). The puncture must be permanently repaired at the earliest opportunity.
