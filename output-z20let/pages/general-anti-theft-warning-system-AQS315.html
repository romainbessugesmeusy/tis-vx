Anti-theft Warning System  

General  

An anti-theft warning system is installed as standard equipment and comprises the following:  

 

•  Control unit.  

•  Movement sensor in the passenger compartment. The sensor is a microwave type.  

•  Switches on the doors, front bonnet and engine cover.  

•  Light emitting diode in the centre console.  

•  Remote control; radio frequency type.  

•  Power sounder (Vauxhall only).
