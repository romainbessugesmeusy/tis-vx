Cylinder Head, Remove and Install (Z20 LET)

  Remove

1.   Release screen wash reservoir from support bracket
•   Slide off
– Position screen wash reservoir to one side



2.   Disconnect battery negative lead
•   Release nut and bolt



3.   Remove engine cover
•   Apply match mark to engine cover 2x hinge
•   Remove 6x bolt, 6x washer, 2x shim
Note:   Shims may vary for cover alignment.
Note:   2x technician required.
4.   Disconnect intercooler hose (1) from connecting pipe for throttle body
•   Release clip (2)
– Position intercooler hose (1) to one side



5.   Disconnect harness connector (1) for secondary oxygen sensor from engine harness
•   Release integral clip
•   Remove, then discard cable tie
•   Lower harness through aperture of left rear inner wheel arch



6.   Raise vehicle
Important:   A four-post hoist must be used to raise the vehicle when removing the engine undertray. The vehicle can be moved to a two-post hoist for other operations if required.
7.   Remove engine undertray (1)
•   Remove 13x bolt, 13x washer
8.   Remove exhaust undertray (2)
•   Remove 12x bolt, 12x washer, 2x clip



9.   Drain coolant
•   Position suitable collecting container
•   Disconnect lower hose (1) from coolant pipe of left side chassis
– Release clip (2)



10.   Remove secondary catalytic converter (1)
•   Remove 6x nut, then discard
•   Release harness for secondary oxygen sensor from chassis
– Release 3x clip
•   Release secondary catalytic converter (1) from rubber mounting and flexible support bracket
•   Remove, then discard gasket



11.   Remove hose (3) from turbocharger (2) and intercooler (1)
•   Release 2x clip
•   Remove, then discard cable tie



12.   Release heat shield for turbocharger from cylinder block
•   Remove bolt (1), washer



Important:   Observe relevant environmental requirements when disposing of used engine oil.
13.   Disconnect oil return pipe for turbocharger
•   Position suitable collecting container
•   Remove pipe banjo bolt (1), 2x seal ring
– Discard 2x seal ring
•   Install 2x blanking plug



14.   Disconnect oil feed pipe from connecting pipe for turbocharger
•   Release union nut (2)
•   Install 2x blanking plug
15.   Release connecting pipe for turbocharger from cylinder block
•   Remove bolt (1)
•   Remove collecting container



16.   Disconnect harness connector (1) from oil level sensor (2)
•   Release integral clip



17.   Release vacuum tank support bracket (2) from intake manifold
•   Remove bolt (1)



18.   Lower vehicle to suitable working height
19.   Remove rear road wheel from right side
•   Remove security bolt
– Use special socket located under front bonnet
•   Remove 4x wheel bolt
20.   Remove right rear wheel arch liner
•   Remove 4x screw, 2x clip
21.   Remove intercooler hose (1) from intercooler
•   Release clip (2)
•   Release fuel vapour line from intercooler hose (1)
– Remove, then discard 4x cable tie (3)



22.   Remove upper front cover (2) for timing belt
•   Remove 2x bolt (1)
•   Pull off to release



23.   Remove ribbed V-belt
•   Release ribbed V-belt tension
– Using suitable spanner rotate tensioner (1) clockwise, then Install suitable locking pin (2)



24.   Remove crankshaft pulley
•   Apply match marks to crankshaft pulley and crankshaft
•   Remove 4x bolt
– Use suitable tool to counter-hold centre bolt on crankshaft sprocket
25.   Remove ribbed V-belt tensioner
•   Remove 2x bolt
26.   Remove lower cover for timing belt
•   Remove 2x bolt (1)



27.   Release rear cover (2) for timing belt
•   Rotate crankshaft in normal direction of rotation until 3x bolt (1) are accessible through camshaft pulleys
•   Remove 3x bolt (1)



28.   Rotate crankshaft in normal direction of rotation to Top Dead Centre (TDC) on cylinder no. 1, until timing marks (2) and (1) are aligned
29.   Lock camshafts in position
•   Use Locking Tool KM-853
30.   Release tension from timing belt
•   Release bolt, then turn adjusting eccentric clockwise in direction until pointer is located just before left stop
31.   Remove timing belt from pulleys
•   Position timing belt to one side



32.   Lower vehicle
33.   Remove air intake hose with engine breather hose
•   Disconnect air intake hose (1) from mass air flow meter (2) and turbocharger (3)
– Release 2x clip
•   Disconnect engine breather hose from cylinder head cover
– Release clip
– Remove, then discard cable tie



34.   Remove support bracket (1) for air intake and engine lifting eye from cylinder head
•   Remove bolt



35.   Remove heat shield (1) from support bracket (2) on cylinder head
•   Remove 4x bolt, 4x washer



36.   Remove support bracket (1) for heat shield from cylinder head
•   Remove bolt, 2x washer
•   Remove engine lifting eye (2)
– Remove bolt



37.   Disconnect harness connector (2) from switchover valve (1) on turbocharger
•   Release integral clip
38.   Disconnect vacuum hose (3) from turbocharger
•   Pull off
39.   Remove guide tube (4) for oil level dipstick from cylinder block
•   Remove oil level dipstick
•   Remove bolt
•   Remove, then discard 2x O ring



40.   Disconnect aeration hose (1) and upper coolant hose (3) from thermostat housing
•   Release 2x clip (2)



41.   Disconnect coolant feed hose (1) from turbocharger, then secure to one side
•   Disconnect pipe banjo (1), 2x seal ring
– Discard 2x seal ring



42.   Disconnect coolant return hose from turbocharger
•   Release clip (1)



43.   Disconnect harness connector for primary oxygen sensor (1) from engine harness
•   Release integral clip
•   Release, then discard cable tie



44.   Release heat shield for turbocharger from transmission housing
•   Remove bolt (1)



45.   Remove exhaust manifold and turbocharger assembly from cylinder head
•   Remove, then discard 10x nut
•   Remove, then discard gasket
•   Clean mating surfaces
46.   Disconnect breather hose (1) from cylinder head cover (3)
•   Release clip (2)



47.   Disconnect vacuum hose (1) from cylinder head cover (2)
•   Release clip (3)



48.   Disconnect vacuum hose (1) from fuel pressure regulator (2)
•   Pull off



49.   Disconnect vacuum hose for switchover valve from throttle body
•   Pull off



50.   Disconnect 2x coolant hose (1) from throttle body
•   Release 2x clip (2)



51.   Disconnect vacuum hose (1) for fuel purge valve from intake manifold
•   Release clip (2)
•   Release clip (3)



52.   Disconnect harness connector (1) from throttle position sensor
•   Release integral clip



53.   Remove throttle body (1) from intake manifold
•   Remove 4x bolt (2)
•   Remove, then discard 2x O ring



Important:   Fuel may escape – observe the safety regulations and relevant national legislation.
54.   Release fuel pressure from fuel system
•   Connect test connection hose (2) to fuel rail
– Remove protective cap
•   Release fuel pressure using Pressure Tester KM-J-34730-91 (1)
– Collect any escaping fuel in a suitable container
55.   Remove Pressure Tester KM-J-34730-91 (1)
•   Disconnect test connection hose (2) from fuel rail
– Install protective cap



56.   Disconnect harness connector (1) from ignition module
•   Release integral clip
57.   Remove cover (2) for ignition module from cylinder head cover
•   Slide off



58.   Disconnect harness connector (1) from camshaft sensor
•   Release integral clip



59.   Release crankshaft position sensor (1) from cylinder block
•   Remove bolt (2)



60.   Release 3x harness from cylinder head cover
•   Release from 3x integral clip
61.   Remove upper support bracket (2) for alternator
•   Remove 3x bolt (1)



62.   Remove side support bracket (2) for alternator
•   Remove 2x bolt (1), washer



63.   Disconnect engine harness from main harness on right side

•   Position fuel rail assembly to one side

•   Release integral clip



68.   Remove intake manifold from vehicle
•   Remove bolt (1), 7x nut (2)
– Discard 7x nut
•   Remove, then discard gasket
•   Clean all mating surfaces



69.   Disconnect coolant hose (1) for oil cooler from coolant bypass housing (3)
•   Release clip (2)



70.   Release harness connector (1) for primary oxygen sensor from harness support bracket (2)
•   Release clip



71.   Release harness support bracket (2) from cylinder head
•   Remove 4x bolt (1)



72.   Disconnect harness connector (1) from temperature switch (2)
•   Release integral clip



73.   Disconnect hose (1) for auxiliary pump from distribution housing
•   Release clip (2)



74.   Remove ignition module (1) from cylinder head cover
•   Remove 2x bolt (2)



75.   Remove cylinder head cover from cylinder head
•   Remove 10x bolt
•   Remove, then discard 10 x O ring, gasket



76.   Remove Locking Tool KM-853 from 2x camshaft pulley
Important:   2nd technician required to counter-hold camshafts.
77.   Remove 2x camshaft pulley (2) and (3)
•   Remove 2x bolt (1), 2x washer
– Discard 2x bolt (1)



Important:   Note position of bearing caps before disassembling. Ensure bearing caps are released from bearing seats evenly in stages of 1/2 to 1 turn in spiral pattern starting outwards to inwards.
78.   Remove intake camshaft (1)
•   Remove 5x bearing cap (2)
– Remove 10x bolt
•   Remove, then discard front oil seal



Important:   Note position of bearing caps before disassembling. Ensure bearing caps are released from bearing seats evenly in stages of 1/2 to 1 turn in spiral pattern starting outwards to inwards.
79.   Remove exhaust camshaft (1)
•   Remove 5x bearing cap (2)
– Remove 10x bolt
•   Remove, then discard front oil seal



Important:   Ensure cylinder head bolts are released from seats evenly in stages of 1/4 turn, then 1/2 turn in spiral pattern starting from centre outwards.
80.   Remove cylinder head from cylinder block
•   Use KM-2355
•   Remove, then discard 10x bolt
81.   Remove, then discard cylinder head gasket
82.   Clean mating surfaces
83.   Check cylinder head for plane surface - see operation “ Cylinder Head, Check for Plane Surface (Z 20 LET) ”

  Install

Important:   Ensure cylinder head gasket is correctly positioned on dowels of cylinder block. Observe direction marks of new gasket, “ OBEN/TOP ” mark uppermost and towards exhaust side of engine.
84.   Install new cylinder head gasket to cylinder block
Important:   Tighten cylinder head bolts in spiral pattern starting from centre outwards.
85.   Install cylinder head to cylinder block
•   Use KM-2355
•   Install, then secure 10x new bolt 25 Nm + 90° + 90° + 90° + 15°
86.   Install new oil seal to exhaust camshaft
•   Lubricate new oil seal
Important:   Do not apply sealant to oil duct area. Lubricate journals and lobes of camshaft with lubricating paste. Install bearing caps to previously marked position. Ensure bearing caps are secured to bearing seats evenly in stages of 1/2 to 1 turn in spiral pattern starting inwards to outwards.
87.   Install exhaust camshaft to cylinder head
•   Install 5x bearing cap
•   Apply sealant to sealing surface of guide bearing
– Install, then secure 10x bolt 8 Nm
88.   Install new oil seal to intake camshaft
•   Lubricate new oil seal
Important:   Lubricate journals and lobes of camshaft with lubricating paste. Install bearing caps to previously marked position. Ensure bearing caps are secured to bearing seats evenly in stages of 1/2 to 1 turn in spiral pattern starting inwards to outwards.
89.   Install intake camshaft to cylinder head

Important:   2nd technician required to counter-hold camshafts.
