Oil Cooler, Replace (Z20 LET)

  Remove

Drain coolant – see operation ”Cooling System, Drain, Fill and Bleed”.

Remove oil drain bolt – collect engine oil.

  Install

Install oil drain bolt with new seal ring – tightening torque 10 Nm .

  Remove

Remove intake manifold – see operation "Intake Manifold, Replace (Z20 LET)".

Disconnect wiring harness plugs for knock sensor (1), ignition module (5), mixture regulating oxygen sensor (3), and ignition coil capacitor (2).

Remove wiring harness plug bracket (4) from coolant flange.


Detach auxiliary coolant pump coolant hose (1) from coolant flange (4) and lay aside.

Remove lower coolant hose from oil cooler.

Detach coolant reservoir coolant hose (3) and heater core (2) from oil cooler and lay aside.


Detach coolant hose (2) from coolant by–pass housing.

Detach coolant hose (3) from coolant pump housing.

Detach oil cooler oil lines (1) from oil cooler – place collecting basin underneath. Remove oil cooler fastening bolts (4) from cylinder block.

Remove oil cooler.


  Install

Insert oil cooler and attach to cylinder block using fastening bolts – tightening torque 20 Nm .

Attach oil lines to oil cooler with new seal rings – tightening torque 30 Nm .

Attach coolant hose to coolant pump housing.

Attach coolant hose to coolant by-pass housing.

Attach coolant compensation tank coolant hose and heater core to oil cooler.

Attach lower coolant hose to oil cooler.

Attach auxiliary coolant pump coolant hose to coolant flange.

Attach wiring harness plug bracket to coolant flange – tightening torque 8 Nm .

Connect wiring harness plugs for knock sensor, ignition module, mixture regulating oxygen sensor, and ignition coil capacitor and route wiring harnesses.

Install intake manifold – see operation "Intake Manifold, Replace (Z20 LET)".

Charge engine oil level up to "MAX"-mark at dipstick.

  Inspect

Charge and bleed cooling system – see operation "Cooling System, Drain, Fill and Bleed".
