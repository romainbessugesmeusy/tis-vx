Engine Oil Temperature, Measure (Z 22 SE, Z 20 LET)

  Remove

Remove oil dipstick.

  Measure

Measure oil temperature with MKM-596-A (1). Insert probe into oil dipstick guide tube up to approx. 1 cm above bottom of oil pan. Seal opening of guide tube with included rubber plug.

Note:   The oil temperature is dependent on the engine load. High ambient temperatures and engine loads can cause oil temperatures of up to approx. 150 °C/302 °F.


  Install

Insert oil dipstick.
