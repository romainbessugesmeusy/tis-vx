Switch, Brake Lamp, Replace

  Remove

Harness connector from brake switch.

Brake switch from pedal box depress brake pedal to release load on switch plunger, push brake switch down towards pedal box, turn through 90° then lift out.

  Install

Brake switch to pedal box, depress brake pedal to give clearance for switch plunger, push brake switch down towards pedal box, then turn through 90° to secure switch to pedal box.

Harness connector to brake switch.
