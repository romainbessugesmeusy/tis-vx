Oil Pump Pressure Control Valve, Remove and Install (Z 22 SE)

  Remove

1.   Open the rear engine cover.
2.   Raise the lifting ramp.
3.   Remove the lower engine splash shield (1).
•   13 bolts
4.   Place a drip tray underneath.



5.   Remove the oil pump pressure control valve.
•   Remove the screw plug (1).
•   Take out the oil seal (2), spring (3) and piston (4).
6.   Check the components.
•   Oil pump pressure control valve
Note:   Check it does not stick.
– Piston, spring



  Install

7.   Install the oil pump pressure control valve.
•   Use a new oil seal.
•   Coat the piston and spring with engine oil and insert them.
•   Install the screw plug.
– Tightening torque 65 Nm
8.   Attach the lower engine splash shield.
•   13 bolts
9.   Lower the lifting ramp.
10.   Check the engine oil level, correct if necessary.
11.   Close the rear engine cover.
