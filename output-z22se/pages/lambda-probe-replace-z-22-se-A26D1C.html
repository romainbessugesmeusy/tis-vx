Lambda Probe, Replace (Z 22 SE)

1.   Remove the lower engine splash shield (1).
•   13 bolts
2.   Open the bonnet.
3.   Disconnect the battery.
4.   Detach the wiring harness connector of the lambda probe for closed–loop mixture control (1).
•   Detach from the bracket.
•   Disconnect the wiring harness connector.
5.   Expose the wiring harness.



6.   Raise the lifting ramp.
7.   Remove the cover from the exhaust system.
•   9 bolts
Important:   Only remove the lambda probe when the engine is at operating temperature.
8.   Remove the lambda probe for closed–loop mixture control (1).
9.   Install the lambda probe for closed–loop mixture control.
•   Transfer the clips.
Note:   When reusing the lambda probe, coat the thread with special grease (black).
•   Tightening torque 40 Nm
10.   Lower the lifting ramp.
11.   Attach the wiring harness connector of the lambda probe for closed–loop mixture control.
•   Route the wiring harness.
•   Connect the wiring harness connector.
•   Attach to bracket.
12.   Connect the battery.
13.   Close the bonnet.
14.   Reprogram the volatile memories.
15.   Attach the cover for the exhaust system.
•   9 bolts
16.   Attach the lower engine splash shield.
•   13 bolts
– Vehicle on a 4–post lifting ramp
