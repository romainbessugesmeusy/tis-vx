Intermediate Pipe, Replace (Z22 SE)

  Install

Chocks to front wheels.

Raise vehicle.

  Remove

Diffuser panel (1) – 14 bolts, 2 clips.


Intermediate pipe from catalytic converter – 3 nuts (1).


  Remove

Intermediate pipe from silencer – clamp (1).

  Install

Intermediate pipe to silencer – clamp (1).

Important:   Do not tighten clamp.


  Install

Intermediate pipe to catalytic converter – 3 nuts (1).

Torque

Clamp for intermediate pipe to silencer – nuts.


  Install

Diffuser panel (1) – 14 bolts, 2 clips.

Lower vehicle.

  Remove

Chocks from front wheels.
