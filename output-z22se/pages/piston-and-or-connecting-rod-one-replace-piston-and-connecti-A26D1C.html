Piston and or Connecting Rod (one), Replace (piston and connecting rod removed)

Note:   Piston and connecting rod removed – see operation "Pistons and Connecting Rods, Remove and Install"

1.   Detach the piston from the connecting rod.
Note:   Note the installation position of the connecting rod and the piston.
•   Remove the snap ring (1).
•   Press out the piston pin.
2.   Attach the piston to the connecting rod.
Important:   The arrow on the piston crown points towards the timing chain end of the engine.
•   Apply engine oil to the piston pin.
•   Press in the piston pin (2).
•   Install the snap ring.



3.   Install the piston rings.
•   Use new piston rings.
Note:   During installation of the second piston ring (Napier ring): "Punkt" (= point or dot) identification faces upwards.
•   Adjust the piston ring gap.
– First piston ring (rectangular–section ring) in position
– Second piston ring (Napier ring) in position
– Intermediate ring of the oil scraper ring in position, steel band rings of the oil scraper ring in position
