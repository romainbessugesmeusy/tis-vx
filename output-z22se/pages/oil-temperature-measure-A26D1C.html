Oil Temperature, Measure

  Remove

Oil dipstick.

  Install

Insert the measuring probe KM-4119-GP from KM-4119 to approx. 1 cm above the floor of the oil pan through the oil dipstick tube.

  Measure

Oil temperature.

The oil temperature depends upon the engine load. At high ambient temperatures or at very high engine speeds the oil temperature may reach approx. 150 °C .

  Remove

Measuring probe.

  Install

Oil dipstick.
