Switch, Reverse Lamp, Replace

1.   Open bonnet
2.   Remove windscreen wash reservoir from mounting bracket
3.   Disconnect battery negative lead
•   Remove nut
4.   Disconnect harness connector (1) from reverse lamp switch



5.   Remove reverse lamp switch from transmission
6.   Install reverse lamp switch to transmission 20 Nm
7.   Secure harness connector to reverse lamp switch
8.   Connect battery negative lead
•   Install nut
9.   Install windscreen wash reservoir to mounting bracket
10.   Close bonnet
11.   Program all volatile memories
