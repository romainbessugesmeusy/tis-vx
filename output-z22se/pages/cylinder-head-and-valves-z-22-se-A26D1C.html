Cylinder Head, Camshaft Drive and Valves  

Cylinder Head and Valves  

The cylinder head is cast from aluminum alloy and there are four valves per cylinder. The valve seat inserts and valve guides are of sintered metal. Oil seals are installed at the valve stems.  

An internal duct is provided at number 4 exhaust port for exhaust gas recirculation purposes.
