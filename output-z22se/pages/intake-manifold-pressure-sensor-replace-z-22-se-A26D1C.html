Intake Manifold Pressure Sensor, Replace (Z 22 SE)

  Remove

1.   Open the bonnet.
2.   Disconnect the battery.
3.   Open the rear engine cover (1).
4.   Detach the engine cover.
•   6 bolts (2) and (3)



5.   Remove the air intake pipe (3).
•   Detach the fuel pressure regulator vacuum hose (2).
•   Detach the intake air temperature sensor wiring harness (1).
•   2 clamps



6.   Detach the throttle body wiring harness connector (1).
•   Release the wiring harness connector.
7.   Remove the throttle body.
•   1 stud, 3 bolts (2)



8.   Detach the wiring harness connector from the intake manifold pressure sensor (2).
9.   Remove the intake manifold pressure sensor (1).



  Install

10.   Install the intake manifold pressure sensor.
11.   Attach the wiring harness connector for the intake manifold pressure sensor.
12.   Install the throttle body.
•   Use a new gasket.
•   Tightening torque 9 Nm
13.   Attach the wiring harness connector for the throttle body.
•   Secure the wiring harness connector(s).
14.   Install the air intake pipe.
•   2 clamps
– Tightening torque 3,5 Nm
•   Attach the intake air temperature sensor wiring harness connector.
•   Attach the fuel pressure regulator vacuum hose.
15.   Install the rear engine cover.
•   6 bolts, tightening torque 14 Nm
16.   Close the rear engine cover.
17.   Connect the battery.
18.   Close the bonnet.
19.   Reprogram the volatile memories.
