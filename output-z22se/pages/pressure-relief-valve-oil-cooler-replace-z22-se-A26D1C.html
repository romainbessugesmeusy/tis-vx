Pressure Relief Valve, Oil Cooler, Replace (Z22 SE)

  Remove

Important:   If securing ring for oil cooler adaptor, becomes loose while removing top cover, then counter-hold using Holding Wrench KM-6416 .
1.   Remove oil filter cover (1) from oil filter housing
•   Use KM-6150
2.   Remove oil filter element from oil filter housing



3.   Remove pressure relief valve (1) from oil filter housing
•   Compress spring, then withdraw



  Install

4.   Install pressure relief valve to oil filter housing
•   Insert valve seat into oil gallery, compress spring, then push valve bracket into position
