Engine, Engine Attaching Parts  

DOHC Petrol Engine Z 22 SE  

The Speedster/VX 220 is available with the Z 22 SE DOHC petrol engine, familiar from the Astra-G. The engine is mounted transversely behind the rear bulkhead.  

Special features of the engine include:  

 

•  Automatic valve adjusters.  

•  Chain-driven camshafts.  

•  Chain-driven balancer shafts.  

•  Chain-driven water pump.  

•  Exhaust gas recirculation duct and valve installed directly in the cylinder head.  

•  Maintenance-free chains.  

 

Changes to the engine for the Speedster/VX 220 include the following:  

 

•  Engine oil dipstick, upper part.  

•  Oil cooler adapter.  

•  Air induction system.  

•  Lift bracket, front.  

•  Fuel pipes and bracket.
