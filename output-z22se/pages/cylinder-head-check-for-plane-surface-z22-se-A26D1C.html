Cylinder Head, Check for Plane Surface (Z22 SE)

1.   Clean the sealing surfaces.
•   Remove any gasket residue.
Important:   The cylinder head must not be skimmed.
2.   Check for plane surface.
•   Check the cylinder head at the sealing surfaces for flexing (across and lengthways) and across the diagonals for distortion.
– Use a straightedge, feeler gauges
Note:   Height of the cylinder head (sealing surface to sealing surface): Dimension (I) – 129 mm
