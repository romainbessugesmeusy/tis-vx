Flywheel, Remove and Install (Z 22 SE, Z 20 LET)

  Remove

Clutch plate and clutch pressure plate – see operation "Clutch Plate and Clutch Pressure Plate, Remove and Install" in Group K.

Flywheel from crankshaft – bolts, immobilise the flywheel with KM-652 .

  Clean

Flywheel bores; flange surfaces of the crankshaft and the flywheel.

  Install

Flywheel to crankshaft – align the centring spigot, immobilise the flywheel with KM-652 , use new bolts, use 53 Nm + 25° to tighten the bolts evenly.

Clutch plate and clutch pressure plate.
