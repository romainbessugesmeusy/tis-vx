Self-diagnosis  

The airbag system is monitored by the fault-finding circuits in the control unit.  

The airbag telltale should illuminate for approximately 4 seconds when the ignition is switched on, then extinguish. If the telltale does not illuminate when the ignition is switched on, or if the telltale illuminates when the vehicle is driven, there is a fault in the system.  

Faults that are detected are stored in the control unit and can be read by means of TECH 2.
