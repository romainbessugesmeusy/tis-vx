Fuel Pump Relay, Remove and Install (Z 22 SE)

1.   Open the bonnet.
2.   Disconnect the battery.
3.   Open the rear engine cover.
4.   Remove the fuel pump relay (1).
•   Pull it out from the socket.
5.   Install the fuel pump relay.
•   Push it into the socket.
– Check that the electrical contacts are good.
6.   Close the rear engine cover.
7.   Connect the battery.
8.   Reprogram the volatile memories.
9.   Close the bonnet.
