Accelerator Pedal, Replace (Z 22 SE)

1.   Open the bonnet.
2.   Disconnect the battery.
3.   Open the driver's door.
4.   Detach the wiring harness connector (1).
•   Release the wiring harness connector in the direction of the arrow.
5.   Remove the accelerator pedal.
•   3 nuts (2)
6.   Install the accelerator pedal.
•   Use new nuts.
•   Tightening torque 12 Nm
7.   Attach the wiring harness connector.
•   Secure the wiring harness connector(s).
8.   Install the interior panelling on the lower instrument panel padding.
•   2 bolts, 2 clips
9.   Close the driver's door.
10.   Connect the battery.
11.   Reprogram the volatile memories.
12.   Close the bonnet.
