Transmission Fluid Level, Check and Correct

Caution !

Check transmission fluid level only after repairs or repair operations on transmission and correct if necessary.

  Inspect

Remove level plug (1), fluid should be level with lower edge of plug aperture.

If necessary: remove filler plug (2) and top up with transmission fluid until fluid escapes from level aperture.

  Install

Level plug to transmission – 35 Nm .

Filler plug to transmission – 35 Nm .
