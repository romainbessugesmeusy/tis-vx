Weatherstrip for Front Roof Frame, Replace

  Remove

1.   Remove soft top or hard top
2.   Remove weatherstrip (1)
•   Cut back head of 2x plastic retainer (2) flush with front roof frame on left side and right side
Note:   Visibly mark position of 2x plastic retainer for later reference.
•   Pull off weatherstrip



3.   Remove all traces of adhesive residue

  Install

4.   Apply masking tape to front roof frame on both top corners adjacent to soft top or hard top mounting brackets
•   Apply tape 200 mm each side of mounting bracket
Note:   This is for marking optimum position of weatherstrip location.
5.   Prime adhesive area
•   Apply recommended primer to complete weatherstrip path
– Allow to cure
6.   Locate new weatherstrip and temporarily tape in position
7.   Install soft top or hard top
8.   Position weatherstrip to optimum sealing position
•   Mark position on previously applied masking tape
9.   Remove soft top or hard top
10.   Punch 2x 5 mm hole in weatherstrip for 2x plastic retainer
Note:   Punch holes should not conflict with position of old plastic retainers.
11.   Partially remove protective tape from weatherstrip
•   Remove protective tape from S section (1) on left side and right side
•   Align weatherstrip to previously marked position and apply pressure



12.   Secure weatherstrip to front roof frame between 2x soft top or hard top mounting brackets
•   Remove protective tape, position weatherstrip then apply pressure
13.   Secure weatherstrip to 2x A pillar
•   Align front weatherstrip (1) with rear weatherstrip (2)
•   Remove protective tape, position weatherstrip then apply pressure



14.   Apply recommended adhesive sealant between front roof frame (1) and weatherstrip (2)
Note:   Apply adhesive sealant along seam where arrowed.



15.   Drill 2x hole for 2x plastic retainer
•   Note position of 2x hole in weatherstrip
•   Use 5 mm drill
•   Drill to maximum depth of 8 mm
16.   Install 2x new plastic retainers
•   Apply recommended adhesive sealant to 2x hole
17.   Apply pressure evenly to weatherstrip, then allow to cure for a minimum of two hours
18.   Install soft top or hard top
