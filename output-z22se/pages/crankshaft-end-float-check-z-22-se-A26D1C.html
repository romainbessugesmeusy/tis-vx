Crankshaft End Float, Check (Z 22 SE)

Note:   Crankshaft and cylinder block base plate attached

1.   Attach the dial gauge MKM-571-B .
•   Attach to one end face of the cylinder block using the bracket.
•   Position and adjust the dual gauge probe on the crankshaft.
2.   Measure the crankshaft end float.
•   Move the crankshaft lengthways.
•   Permissible crankshaft end float: 0,092 - 0,24 mm
3.   Detach the dial gauge MKM-571-B .
