Bearing, Drive Shaft in Clutch Housing, Replace

1.   Illustration of drive shaft
1. Spacer sleeve
2. Drive shaft bearing




2.   Remove transmission from vehicle- see operation "Transmission, Remove and Install"
3.   Disassemble transmission - see operation "Transmission, Seal Completely"
4.   Remove drive shaft bearing (4) and spacer sleeve (3) from clutch housing with KM-248-2 (1) and KM-248-4 (2)



5.   Clean all removed parts and check for wear and damage; replace if necessary
6.   Install drive shaft bearing (3) into clutch housing with KM-6120 (2) in conjunction with KM-523-1 (1). Rounded-off side (arrows) of drive shaft bearing points to spacer sleeve
7.   Install spacer sleeve (4) into clutch housing with KM-6112 (2) in conjunction with KM-523-1 (1)



8.   Assemble transmission - see operation "Transmission, Seal Completely"
9.   Install transmission into vehicle- see operation "Transmission, Remove and Install"
