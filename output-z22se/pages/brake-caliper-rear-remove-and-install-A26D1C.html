Brake Caliper, Rear, Remove and Install

  Remove

Wheel from vehicle – bolts.

Parking brake cable from brake caliper.

Brake caliper from support bracket – M10 bolt and M8 screw; move brake caliper to one side.


  Install

Brake caliper to support bracket – M10 bolt and M8 screw; use suitable material to lock threads.

Parking brake cable to brake caliper.

Wheel to vehicle – bolts 90 Nm .

  Adjust

Parking brake – see operation "Parking Brake, Check and Adjust".
