Rear Subframe, Remove and Install (Z22 SE)

  Remove

Rear clamshell – see operation "Rear Clamshell, Remove and Install".

Wiring harnesses of both oxygen sensors – separate into individual cables; 2 wiring harness plugs (arrowed), cable ties.

Wiring harnesses of both rear wheel speed sensors – separate into individual cables; 1 wiring harness plug (arrowed), cable ties on each side.

Front exhaust pipe (2) from exhaust manifold (1) – 3 nuts

Left exhaust pipe mounting (arrowed) – 1 screw.

Exhaust system – 3 mountings (arrowed).

Parking brake cable (1) from compensator (2) – 1 locking pin (4), 1 pin (3).

Parking brake cables (1) from lever (2) – hang parking brake cables on left and right sides of vehicle.

Parking brake cables (3) from lower wishbone (4) – detach one bracket (5) on each side.

  Remove

Rear brake hoses and rear brake lines (arrowed) from mountings on left and right.

Important:  

Collect and dispose of escaping brake fluid.

Follow safety regulations.

  Remove

Cover (1) – 1 screw.

  Remove

Left lower wishbone (1) from chassis frame and rear sub–frame – 1 screw, 1 nut (arrowed).

Right lower wishbone (1) from chassis frame and rear sub–frame – 1 screw, 1 nut.

Important:   Proceed with care so as not to damage thread of track rod end (2).

  Remove

Both drive shafts from transmission – see operation "Drive Shaft, Remove and Install" in group F.

  Remove

Left suspension assembly – 3 screws (arrowed).

Important:   Collect and dispose of escaping transmission fluid.

  Install

Blanking plug into left drive shaft aperture.

  Remove

Right suspension assembly – 3 screws (arrowed).

Important:   Collect and dispose of escaping transmission fluid.

  Install

Blanking plug into right drive shaft aperture.

  Remove

Rear engine damping block (1) – 4 screws.

Screws from sub–frame at bottom (arrowed).

Fuel cutoff switch (1) from sub–frame – 2 screws

Attach engine to crane – 2 attachment points (arrowed).

Air filter bracket (1) – 2 screws, 1 nut.

Left bracket (3) –3 screws, 1 nut.

Left engine damping block (2), 1 nut.

Right bracket (4) – 3 screws, 1 nut, 1 fuel line mounting.

Right engine damping block (5), 1 nut.

  Remove

Screws from sub–frame at top (arrowed).

Important:   Do not lean or place any weight on rear sub–frame after removal of rear sub–frame upper screws.

  Remove

Rear sub–frame (2) from support arms (1) – 1 screw (arrowed) on each side.

2 extra technicians required to remove rear sub–frame.

Rear Sub–frame, Install

  Install

Rear sub–frame (1) loosely to support arms (2) – 1 screw (arrowed) on each side.

2 extra technicians required to install rear sub–frame.

  Install

Upper screws (1 and 2) loosely into rear sub–frame.

Lower screws (3 and 4) loosely into rear sub–frame.

Important:   Use new self–locking nuts.

Torque

Rear sub–frame upper screws (1 and 2)– 45 Nm .

Rear sub–frame lower screws (3 and 4)– 45 Nm .

  Install

Support arms of roll bar to rear sub–frame – tighten 1 screw (arrowed) on each side 45 Nm .

  Install

Left engine damping block (2), 1 nut 45 Nm .

Left bracket (3) –3 screws 45 Nm , 1 nut.

Right engine damping block (5), –1 nut 45 Nm .

Right bracket (4) –3 screws 85 Nm , 1 nut.

Air filter bracket (1) – 2 screws, 1 nut.

Important:  

Use new self–locking nuts.

Carefully check installation position of engine damping blocks.

  Install

Rear engine damping block (1) – 4 screws.

Torque

Screw, rear engine damping block to transmission – 86 Nm .

Screw, rear engine damping block to rear sub–frame – 49 Nm .

  Remove

Crane from engine (arrowed).

Fuel cutoff switch (1) to rear sub–frame – 2 screws

  Install

Retaining ring (arrowed) to both drive shafts.

  Remove

Blanking plugs from drive shaft apertures.

  Install

Right drive shaft into transmission.

Right upper wishbone and shock absorber – 3 screws (arrowed); ensure screw of correct length 55 mm (measured from screw base to screw head) is installed in upper wishbone.

Important:   Use new self–locking nuts.

Torque

Screw, shock absorber to rear sub–frame – 45 Nm .

Screw, upper wishbone to rear sub–frame – 45 Nm .

  Install

Left drive shaft into transmission.

Left upper wishbone and shock absorber – 3 screws (arrowed); ensure screw of correct length 55 mm (measured from screw base to screw head) is installed in upper wishbone.

Important:   Use new self–locking nuts.

Torque

Screw, shock absorber to rear sub–frame – 45 Nm .

Screw, upper wishbone to rear sub–frame – 45 Nm .

  Install

Brake hoses and lines (arrowed) to mounting on each side.

Wiring harnesses of both rear wheel speed sensors – 1 wiring harness plug (arrowed) and 2 cable ties on each side.

Parking brake cable (1) to lever (2) – attach to both sides.

Parking brake cables (3) to lower wishbone (4) – one bracket (5) on each side.

  Install

Important:   End of track control arm locates into chassis. Ensure correct type of locking nut is used. Early type have coarse thread, later type have fine thread.

Left lower wishbone (1) – 1 screw 45 Nm and 1 nut (arrow) 49 Nm ; ensure screw of correct length, 65 mm (measured from screw base to screw head) is installed.

Right lower wishbone (1) – 1 screw 45 Nm and 1 nut (arrow) 49 Nm ; ensure screw of correct length, 65 mm (measured from screw base to screw head) is installed.

Important:   Use new self–locking nuts.

Exhaust system – 3 mountings (arrowed).

Important:   Use new nuts.
