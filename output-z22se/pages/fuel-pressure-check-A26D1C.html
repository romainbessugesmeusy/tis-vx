Fuel Pressure, Check

Warning!

Fuel may escape – observe the safety regulations and relevant national legislation.

  Install

Fuel pressure test gauge KM-J-34730-91 to test connection (1).

Start the engine and bleed the fuel pressure test gauge. Collect any escaping fuel in a suitable container.

  Inspect

Fuel pressure. Start the engine and run at idling speed.

  Remove

Fuel pressure test gauge from test connection (1).
