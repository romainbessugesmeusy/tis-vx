Complaint: High oil consumption.
Cause: Tangential force of piston rings at lower limit or out of specification.
Production: Piston rings with correct tangential force have been introduced as of the following engine no's: Z22YH: Engine-no. 11301385 (09.12.2003) Z22SE: Engine-no. 11307049 (08.01.2004)

Remedy:

In case of complaint, carry out oil measuring procedure according TIS 2000
first (document 00030907).
Only if oil consumption exceeds the specification replace piston rings on
complaint engines.
