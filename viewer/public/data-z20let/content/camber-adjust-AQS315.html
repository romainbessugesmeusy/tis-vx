Camber, Adjust

Camber angle (negative) is controlled by shims positioned between the steering arm and hub carrier. Shims are available in 1 mm and 3 mm thicknesses.

  Measure

Camber using optical alignment measuring equipment. For dimensions refer to “Technical Data”

  Adjust

Camber angle - to decrease negative camber towards zero, add shims. To increase negative camber away from zero, remove shims - 1 mm shim will alter camber by approximately 0.25° .

Torque

Steering arm to hub carrier bolts - 45 Nm .
