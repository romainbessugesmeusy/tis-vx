Field Remedy: 1698

Subject: Engine cover - Water ingress
Models: Engines: Option:
Speedster 2003,VX220 2003 Z20LET,Z20LET
Complaint: Corrosion of spark plugs / ignition malfunction.
Cause: Humidity in spark shaft.
Production: As of VIN W0L0EBR973N007192 (05 November 2003) a water deflector is installed at plant to the underside of the hood.

Remedy:


In case of customer complaint install a water deflector to the underside of
the hood.

Note:


     - The water deflector has to be installed into the groove of the hood on
       the right side.
     - The holes for fastening of water deflector have to be drilled into the
       outer bar of the groove only.
     - When mounting the water deflector, take care that the holes of water
       deflector will be aligned to the holes in the bar of the hood using a
       suitable tool.
       If the holes will not be aligned to each other, the water deflector
       will be pressed and clamped to the inner bar of the groove.

Working Procedure:


 1. Cover engine and rear fender against dirt and/or damage
    (Picture I, 1 and 2).







 2. Measure and mark position of water deflector to hood (Picture II).




    Measure "I" =   15.5 cm







 3. Mask the hood in the working area for protection against damage with
    fibre band (Picture III, 1).







 4. Measure and mark position of holes for fastening water deflector to hood
    outside.

Note:


     Use the holes in the water deflector as template.




 5. Drill holes for fastening water deflector to the hood outside
    (Picture IV, 1).

Note:


     - The water deflector has to be installed into the groove of the hood on
       the right side.
     - The holes for fastening of water deflector have to be drilled into the
       outer bar of the groove only.
     - Diameter of bore = 3.5 mm







 6. Cut threads into the holes at the water deflector.

Note:


     Use the fastening screws to cut the threads.




 7. Install the water deflector with both screws to the hood (Picture V).

Note:


     Align the holes of water deflector to the holes of the bores in the hood
     with a suitable tool (1).







 8. Mark position of the holes for fastening of water deflector to the hood
    in the middle (Picture VI, 1).







 9. Remove water deflector.
10. Pre-drill the holes for fastening water deflector to the hood in the
    middle.

Important:


     Drill carefully – hood is made of glass fibre plastic.

Note:


     Diameter of bore = 3.5 mm




11. Ream the holes for fastening the water deflector to the hood
    in the middle.

Note:


     Diameter of bore = 12.5 mm




12. Deburr the holes.
13. Install 2x cavity dowels to the hood in the middle (Picture VII, 1).







14. Install water deflector to the hood with four screws
    (Picture VIII, 1 and 2).

Note:


     Tightening torque screws (2) = 3.5 Nm.







15. Remove the covers from engine and rear fender.
16. Remove fibre band from hood.
17. Clean engine compartment, hood and rear fender.
