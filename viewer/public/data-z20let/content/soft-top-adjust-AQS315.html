Soft Top, Adjust

  Adjust

1.   Open windows and doors
2.   Release cant rail latches on left side, then disengage soft top from brackets
•   Release 2x latch



3.   Roll soft top cant rail towards centre of vehicle



4.   Adjust tension of rear cable
•   Release lock nut (1)
•   Adjust tension as required, then secure lock nut
5.   Repeat operation for front cable if required
