Field Remedy: 1959

Subject: Engine Z20LET, Turbocharger - Modified cooling
Models: Engines: Option:
Speedster 2003...2005,VX220 2003...2005 Z20LET,Z20LET
Complaint: Cooling connection of the new P&A turbocharger (part-no. 55559848 / catalogue-no.: 58 490 40) does not fit to Speedster/VX220.
Cause: Modified turbocharger cooling.
Production: Introduction of the new turbocharger cooling as of MY2006 on Z20LER/LEL/LET engines.

Remedy:

In case of replacement of the turbocharger (part-no.: 55559848 / catalogue-no.: 58 490 40)on Speedster/VX220 the new P&A turbocharger has to be modified as follows.

Remove the upper cooling pipe. Dismount the plug (mounted sidewards on bearing housing) and install it with a new sealring instead of the cooling pipe. Sealring part-no.: 11016282 / catalogue-no.: 20 91 057.

FunctionalGroup: J - Engine
Complaint Group: 24 - Wrong or not mounted
Trouble Code: None
