Headlamps, Exterior Lamps, Interior Lamps  

Front Lamp Assembly  

The front lamp assembly is equipped with the following:  

 

•  Headlamp.  

•  Turn signal lamp.  

•  Parking lamp.  



 

• Service  

Access to the bulbs in each front lamp assembly is gained after removal of the front lamp assembly, which is secured to the front clamshell by means of a wing nut.
