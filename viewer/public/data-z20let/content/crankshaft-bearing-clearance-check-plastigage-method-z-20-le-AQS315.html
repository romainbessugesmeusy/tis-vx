Crankshaft Bearing Clearance, Check (Plastigage Method), (Z 20 LET)

  Inspect

Bearing play - bearing cover removed.

Caution

Clean crankshaft journal and crankshaft bearing shell and lubricate slightly so that thread does not tear when bearing cap is removed. Do not turn crankshaft.

  Measure

With "Plastigage" (deformable plastic thread).

Cut strip to width of bearing and lay axially between crankshaft bearing journal and bearing shell (arrow). Attach bearing cap with torque - tightening torque 50 Nm + 45° + 15° .

Note:   The bolts can be reused for checking crankshaft bearing play.


  Remove

Remove bearing cover.

  Measure

Compare width of flattened plastic thread (arrow) with measuring scale.

"Plastigage" is available for various measuring ranges.

Perm. crankshaft bearing play: 0.015 to 0.043 mm.

Note:   When reading the value, do not confuse millimetres with inches on the measuring scale.

Torque-angle method

Attach crankshaft bearing cap to cylinder block with new bolts - 50 Nm + 45° + 15° .
