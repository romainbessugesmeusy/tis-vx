Z 20 LET Petrol Engine

The Speedster/VX220 Turbo is equipped with the Z 20 LET turbocharged, intercooled, petrol engine. This is the same turbocharged two litre engine that is installed in the Astra Coupé, Turbo Edition, but with minor changes. Whilst the ancillary drive belt layout remains the same, the air conditioning compressor is removed and replaced with a dummy pulley assembly (1).



There is a modified inlet casting (1) for the throttle body to accomodate the new installation.

The turbocharger is equipped with a two-piece heat shield (2) to funnel the hot air out of the engine compartment.
