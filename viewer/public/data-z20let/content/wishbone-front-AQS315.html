Wishbones, Front  

The two wishbones are fabricated from steel tube. The inboard ends of the wishbones are equipped with bonded rubber bushes and a swivel joint is located in the outer end of each wishbone.  

A nitrile-faced washer prevents metal to metal contact at the upper wishbone under extreme braking force. This washer is installed at the rear face of the front bush.
