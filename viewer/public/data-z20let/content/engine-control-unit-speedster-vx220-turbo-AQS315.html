Engine Control Unit - Speedster/VX220 Turbo

The engine control unit for Speedster/VX220 Turbo is the Motronic ME 1.5.5 unit as used for the Astra Coupé, Turbo Edition. It is secured to the rear clamshell on the left side of the engine. The unit has been re-programmed with new algorithms for use in the Speedster/VX220 Turbo.
