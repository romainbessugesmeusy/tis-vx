Requirements at the measurement point

The measurement point must meet certain requirements to allow precise alignment and setting. The reproducibility of measurement values is also ensured in this way.

It is very important that the wheel contact points of the measurement plate are height-aligned to each other. This is usually checked with a visual levelling device.

The permitted height difference between the left and right side is max. 1 mm, between front and rear and diagonal max. 2 mm. The lift must be adjustable in three working heights with this dimensional accuracy.
