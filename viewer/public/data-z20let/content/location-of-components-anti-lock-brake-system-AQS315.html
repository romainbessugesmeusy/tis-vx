Location of Components  

The illustration shows the location of components in a left drive vehicle. The hydraulic modulator and control unit is located on the right side on right drive vehicles.  

 


 

 

 

 

1  ABS telltale  

2  Switch, stop lamp  

3  Hydraulic modulator and control unit  

4  Speed sensor, front wheel  

 

The rear wheel speed sensor is similar to that at the front wheel.
