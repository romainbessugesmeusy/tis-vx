Parking Brake Cable, Rear, Replace

  Remove

Rear undertray (1) – 13 bolts, 13 washers.

Diffuser panel (2) – 7 bolts, 2 clips.


Fully release parking brake lever.

  Remove

Parking brake cable (3) from brake caliper (1) and lever (2) on each side of vehicle – slacken locknuts.

Parking brake cable (3) from support brackets (4 and 5) - slacken locknuts.

Parking brake cables (7 and 3) from compensator (6) – clip and pin.

  Clean

All components.


Lubricate components with suitable grease.

  Install

Parking brake cables (3 and 7) to compensator (6) – pin and clip.

Parking brake cable (3) to support brackets (4 and 5) - tighten locknuts.

Parking brake cable (3) to brake caliper (1) and lever (2) on each side of vehicle – tighten locknuts.

  Adjust

Parking brake – see operation "Parking Brake, Check and Adjust".


  Install

Diffuser panel (2) – 7 bolts, 2 clips.

Important:   Do not tighten bolts.

  Install

Rear undertray (1) – 13 bolts, 13 washers.

Tighten diffuser and undertray bolts.
