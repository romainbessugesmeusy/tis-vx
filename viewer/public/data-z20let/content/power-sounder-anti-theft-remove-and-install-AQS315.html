Power Sounder, Anti-Theft, Remove and Install

  Remove

Slacken nuts that hold power sounder to bracket.

Ease power sounder from bolts in bracket and pivot pip.

Harness connector from power sounder.

  Install

Harness connector to power sounder.

Power sounder to bracket, locate bolts heads into slots on sounder and pivot pip in bracket – tighten nuts.
