Crankshaft Out-of-round Check (Z 22 SE, Z 20 LET)

1.   Crankshaft removed
Note:   Crankshaft removed – see operation "Crankshaft, Remove and Install"
2.   Lay the crankshaft in the cylinder block.
3.   Attach the dial gauge MKM-571-B .
•   Attach with the bracket to the cylinder block.
•   Position and adjust the dual gauge probe on the crankshaft bearing journal.
4.   Check the radial run–out of the crankshaft.
•   Turn the crankshaft smoothly.
•   Max. permissible radial run–out: 0,03 mm
5.   Detach the dial gauge MKM-571-B .
