Coolant Flange, Replace (Z20 LET)

  Remove

Drain coolant – see operation ”Cooling System, Drain, Fill and Bleed”

Disconnect wiring harness plugs for knock sensor (1), ignition module (5), mixture regulating oxygen sensor (3), and ignition coil capacitor (2). Remove wiring harness plug bracket (4) from coolant flange.

Detach or remove wiring harness plug from coolant temperature sensor, exhaust gas recirculation valve, oxygen sensor and DIS ignition module.


Remove fastening bolts (1) and remove coolant flange.

  Clean

Clean sealing surfaces and remove gasket residues.


  Install

Attach coolant flange to cylinder head with new gasket – tightening torque (M8) 20 Nm , (M10) 30 Nm .

Attach wiring harness plug bracket to coolant flange – tightening torque 8 Nm .

Attach or connect wiring harness plug to coolant temperature sensor, exhaust gas recirculation valve, oxygen sensor and to DIS ignition module.

Connect and clip in wiring harness plugs for knock sensor, ignition module, mixture regulating oxygen sensor, and ignition coil capacitor and route wiring harnesses.

Charge and bleed cooling system – see operation "Cooling System, Drain, Fill and Bleed”
