Exhaust Manifold Gasket, Replace (Z 22 SE)

  Remove

1.   Open the bonnet.
2.   Disconnect the battery.
3.   Open the engine cover (1).
4.   Detach the engine cover.
•   6 bolts (2) and (3)



5.   Release the fuel pressure.
•   Warning!
Fuel may escape – observe the safety regulations and relevant national legislation.
•   Release the fuel pressure with the pressure tester KM-J-34730-91 via test connection (2).
– Collect any escaping fuel in a suitable container.



6.   Disconnect the fuel lines.
•   2 union nuts (1)
•   2 bolts (2)
•   Reposition the fuel lines (3) to one side.



7.   Detach the ignition module (1).
•   Release and detach the wiring harness connector.
•   4 bolts (2)



8.   Remove the cylinder head cover.
•   Detach the engine breather hose (2).
•   Detach the engine lifting eyes (5) and (6).
– 2 bolts, 1 nut
•   Detach the ground cable (7).
– 1 bolt
•   12 bolts (1), (3), (4) and (8)



9.   Remove the lower heat shield (1).
•   3 bolts
10.   Detach the upper heat shield (2).
•   3 bolts
11.   Raise the lifting ramp.



12.   Detach the rear left–hand wheel.
13.   Detach the rear left–hand wheel arch trim.
•   6 bolts
14.   Disconnect the lambda probe wiring harness connector (2).
•   Expose the wiring harness.
•   5 clips, 1 retaining clip (1)



15.   Raise the lifting ramp.
16.   Remove the lower engine splash shield (1).
•   13 bolts



17.   Detach the front exhaust pipe.
•   3 nuts (1)
•   Remove the gasket.
18.   Lower the lifting ramp.



19.   Remove the exhaust manifold (1).
•   10 nuts.
•   Remove the gasket (2).
20.   Clean the sealing surfaces.
•   Exhaust manifold, cylinder head, front exhaust pipe.



  Install

21.   Install the exhaust manifold.
•   Use a new gasket.
•   Use new nuts.
– Tightening torque 12 Nm
•   Wait for 30 seconds.
– Tightening torque 12 Nm
22.   Raise the lifting ramp.
23.   Attach the front exhaust pipe.
•   Use a new gasket.
•   Tightening torque 16 Nm
24.   Attach the lower engine splash shield.
•   13 bolts
25.   Lower the lifting ramp.
26.   Connect the lambda probe wiring harness connector.
•   Route the wiring harness.
27.   Install the wheel arch trim.
•   6 bolts
28.   Attach the rear left–hand wheel.
•   Do not tighten yet.
29.   Lower the lifting ramp.
30.   Tighten the rear left–hand wheel.
•   Tightening torque 90 Nm
31.   Attach the upper heat shield.
•   Attach the engine lifting eye.
– 1 nut
•   3 studs
•   Tightening torque 23 Nm
32.   Install the lower heat shield.
•   Tightening torque 8 Nm
33.   Detach the ground cable from the cylinder head.
•   1 bolt
34.   Attach the cylinder head cover
•   Clean the sealing surfaces.
•   Use new gaskets.
Note:   The sealing lips of the seals (1) must be inserted into the groove on the cylinder head cover.
– Tightening torque 9 Nm
•   Attach the ground cable
– Tightening torque 9 Nm
•   Attach the engine lifting eyes.
– 2 bolts, 1 nut
35.   Attach the engine breather hose.
36.   Attach the fuel line bracket.
•   Tightening torque 8 Nm
37.   Attach the ignition module.
•   Tightening torque 9 Nm
•   Attach and secure the wiring harness connector.



38.   Connect the fuel lines.
•   2 union nuts
– Tightening torque 10 Nm
•   2 bolts
– Tightening torque 9 Nm
39.   Install the rear engine cover.
•   Check the alignment.
•   6 bolts, tightening torque 14 Nm
40.   Close the rear engine cover.
41.   Connect the battery.
42.   Close the bonnet.
43.   Reprogram the volatile memories.
