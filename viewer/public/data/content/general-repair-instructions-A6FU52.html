General Repair Instructions

The exhaust system is to be installed free of tension and aligned according to the contours of the vehicle underbody.

Check all components for signs of wear and replace if necessary.

Check exhaust system for leaks.

When replacing the exhaust system or individual components, use only parts that are suitable for the vehicle (observe appropriate catalogue No. and part No.).

If the oxygen sensor is to be re-used, coat the thread with special grease (black). On a new oxygen sensor, the thread is already coated with special grease (black).
