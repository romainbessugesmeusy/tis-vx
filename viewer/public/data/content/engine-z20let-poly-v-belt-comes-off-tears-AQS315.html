Subject: Engine Z20LET - Poly-V-belt comes off / tears
Models: Engines: Option:
VX220 2003...,Speedster 2003... Z20LET,Z20LET
Complaint: Poly-V-belt comes off / tears.
Cause: On Speester Z20LET without A/C an idler is installed instead of the A/C compressor. The idler bracket design causes an incorrect Poly-V- belt alignment.
Production: Implementation of a modified pulley bracket (moving the belt forward by 3.6mm) as of the following VIN/Engine-no.: BP: W0L0EBR973N007200 Engine-no.: Z20LET31024880; Date: 19.11.2003

Remedy:

In case of customer complaint repalce Poly-V-belt and install the improved
pulley bracket (new Part-No.: 93183526 / Cat-No: 48 01 695).
FunctionalGroup: J - Engine,N - Electrical Equipment
Complaint Group: 01 - Broken or Cracked
Trouble Code: None
