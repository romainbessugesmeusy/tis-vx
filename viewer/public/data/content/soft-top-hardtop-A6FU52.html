Soft Top, Hardtop  

A soft top is available as standard; a hardtop is available as an option. Vehicles that are equipped with a hardtop are also supplied with a soft top.  

The soft top is attached to the windscreen frame, side header rails and rear header rail. The side header rails are locked in position by means of latches and are removed with the soft top for stowage in a bag.  

Quick release fasteners secure the soft top to the rear header rail.  

The bag is stowed behind the seats.  



The hardtop is attached to the windscreen frame by means of three bolts and to the rear header rail by means of two quick release fasteners.
