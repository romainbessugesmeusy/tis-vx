General Information

The “Wiring Diagram” application provides a linked set of information that was previously available in the Vehicle Electrical Folder and Checking Procedures. This information includes: block diagrams, circuit diagrams, connector end views, 2D and 3D harness views, grounding point views, fuse locations, Tech 31/32 information, Wiring Harness Repair and troubleshooting instructions.

In the following chapters the function of the circuit diagram is explained.

  • 1. General

    • In this chapter it is explained what information is supplied with the wiring diagram application and how it is designed to be used.

  • 2. Circuit Diagrams

    • The circuit diagrams are drawn with a certain philosophy and consistency behind them, which, once familiar with them, provide easy reading of the diagrams and quick access to the required information.
    In this chapter the philosophy of the circuit diagrams is given.

  • 3. Components

    • Throughout the circuit diagrams many different components can be identified; all indicated with their own symbol. These symbols can provide a lot of information on the component itself such as their functionality, operating principle etc..
    In this chapter the philosophy behind component symbols is given.
