Piston or Con-Rod, Replace (Z20 LET)

  Remove

Remove piston and con-rod – see operation "Piston and Con-rod, Remove and Install".

Before removing piston pin, note installation position of con-rod and piston. Remove retaining ring (1) from piston boss with suitable tool. Remove piston pin from piston and con-rod by hand.

  Install

Coat piston pin with engine oil. Press piston pin (2) by hand into piston and con-rod until ring groove is accessible. Insert retaining ring in ring groove of piston.

Install piston and con-rod – see operation "Piston and Con-rod, Remove and Install".
