Instrument Cluster – Speedster/VX220 Turbo

The new instrument cluster has the following features:

  • New silver background
  • New lettering font
  • Speed range for speedometer increased to 260 km/h
  • Symbols aligned to current ISO 7000 standard
  • New fuel gauge, consisting of a horizontal bar of LCD segments
  • Warning lamp for brake failure (brake fluid level low) to combine function for parking brake engaged
  • New warning lamp for seat belt engagement
  • Alarm warning lamp combined with “change-up” warning lamp”
