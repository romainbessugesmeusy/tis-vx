Parking Brake Cable, Front, Replace

  Remove

Rear undertray (1) – 13 bolts, 13 washers.

Diffuser panel (2) – 7 bolts, 2 clips.


Fully release parking brake lever.

  Remove

Parking brake trim from parking brake lever.

Parking brake cable (1) from parking brake lever – clip and pin.

Parking brake cable (1) from compensator – clip and pin.

Parking brake cable (1) from underbody.

  Clean

All components.


Lubricate components with suitable grease.

  Install

Parking brake cable (1) to compensator – pin and clip.

Parking brake cable (1) to parking brake lever – pin and clip.

Parking brake trim to parking brake lever.

  Adjust

Parking brake – see operation "Parking Brake, Check and Adjust".


  Install

Diffuser panel (2) – 7 bolts, 2 clips.

Important:   Do not tighten bolts.

  Install

Rear undertray (1) – 13 bolts, 13 washers. Tighten diffuser and undertray bolts.
