Cylinder Head, Check for Plane Surface (Z 20 LET)

  Clean

Remove gasket residues and clean sealing surfaces.

  Inspect

Cylinder head dismantled.

Check length and width of cylinder head sealing surfaces for deformation and diagonals for warpage - use straight edge.

Caution

Resurfacing of the cylinder head is not permitted.

  Measure

Measure height of cylinder head (sealing surface to sealing surface): dimension (I) - 134 mm.
