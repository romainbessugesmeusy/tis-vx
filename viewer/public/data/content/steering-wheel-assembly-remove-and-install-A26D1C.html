Steering Wheel Assembly, Remove and Install

Important:   Observe safety regulations during operations on the airbag.

  Remove

Driver's airbag – see operation "Driver's Airbag, Remove and Install" in group C.


Caution !

For removal of steering wheel, steering column must be in straight ahead position. In this position, remove ignition key and turn steering wheel to left or right until steering lock engages.

  Remove

Airbag contact unit from steering wheel assembly – bolts (1).

Steering wheel assembly from steering column – nut (2); use Puller KM-210-B .


  Install

Steering wheel assembly to steering column – nut (2) 25 Nm .

Contact unit to steering wheel assembly – bolts (1).


Driver's airbag – see operation "Driver's Airbag, Remove and Install" in group C.
