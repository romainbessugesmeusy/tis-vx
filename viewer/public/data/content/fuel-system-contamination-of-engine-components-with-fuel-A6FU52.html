Field Remedy: 2330

Subject: Fuel system - Contamination of engine components with fuel
Models: Engines: Option:
All All
Complaint: Contamination of engine components with fuel causes different defects.
Cause: Working on the open fuel system e.g. replacement of fuel filter element or parts of the fuel system.
Production:

Remedy:

When working on the open fuel system ensure that escaping fuel gets
collected in suitable containers. Please make sure that engine and 
vehicle components e.g. starter does not come into contact with 
escaping fuel.


Note: Escaping fuel must be disposed immediately and soiled components 
      shall be cleaned.
FunctionalGroup: L - Fuel- and Exhaust System
Complaint Group:
Trouble Code: None
