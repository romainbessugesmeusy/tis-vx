Charge Pressure Regulator, Solenoid Valve, Replace (Z 20 LET)

  Remove

Disconnect wiring harness plug (4) from charge pressure regulator solenoid valve (3).

Detach 3x vacuum hose (1) from charge pressure regulator solenoid valve.

Remove 2x bolt (2) from charge pressure regulator solenoid valve and remove upwards.

  Install

Attach charge pressure regulator solenoid valve to turbocharger.

Attach vacuum hoses to charge pressure regulator solenoid valve.

Connect wiring harness plug to charge pressure regulator solenoid valve.
