Fuel Filter, Replace

Caution !

Fuel escapes – observe safety measures and national legislation.

  Remove

Air cleaner assembly – see operation "Air Cleaner Assembly, Remove and Install" in group J.


Fuel lines from fuel filter – close off fuel lines with Closure Plug KM-807 .

Fuel filter from bulkhead – bolt (1).

  Install

Fuel filter to bulkhead – bolt (1) 9 Nm .

Fuel lines to fuel filter – use new seals.

Important:   Observe direction of flow.


  Install

Air cleaner assembly – see operation "Air Cleaner Assembly, Remove and Install" in group J.

  Inspect

Start engine and check for leaks.
