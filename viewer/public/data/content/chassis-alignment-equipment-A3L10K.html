Chassis alignment equipment

For Opel/Vauxhall vehicles, dealers are currently recommended the following computer-supported chassis alignment systems, the technical data of which is given in the table below. These manufacturers also get technical support from Opel/Vauxhall.

  • Beissbarth, Microline 5000 Opel or Vauxhall
  • Hunter, Opel alignment machine
  • Hofmann Werkstatt-Technik, Geoliner 482 Opel or Vauxhall
  • John Bean, Visualiner 901 Opel or Vauxhall 1)
1 ) Machine virtually identical to the Hofmann Geoliner 482 Opel
