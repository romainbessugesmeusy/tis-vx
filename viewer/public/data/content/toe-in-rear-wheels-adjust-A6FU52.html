Toe-in, Rear Wheels, Adjust

  Measure

Rear wheel toe-in using optical alignment measuring equipment. For dimensions refer to “Technical Data”

  Adjust

Toe-in if necessary:

Loosen locknuts (1) and (2) on both right and left tie rods. Always adjust both tie rods same amount to obtain setting. Tighten locknuts on both tie rods - 45 Nm .

  Measure

Toe-in setting, final check.
