Undertray, Remove and Install  

  Important!   

Follow vehicle lifting instructions given in Introduction.  

With undertray installed, vehicle cannot be lifted by means of a two post hoist placed under chassis frame.  

To remove undertray, vehicle should be lifted by means of a four post hoist or a workshop jack.  

To remove undertray by means of a workshop jack, vehicle should be lifted on one side by placing a workshop jack at marked jacking points (arrowed).  

Vehicle must be secured by fitting one safety stand (1) at front jacking point (arrowed) and a second safety stand at rear below rear lower wishbone (2).  

 

-  Secure vehicle to prevent vehicle rolling away.  

-  Only use authorised safety stands.  

-  Do not damage bottom of vehicle.  

-  Do not damage axle parts.
