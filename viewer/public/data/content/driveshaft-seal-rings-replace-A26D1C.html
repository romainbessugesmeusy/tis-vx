Driveshaft Seal Rings, Replace

  Remove

Driveshaft from transmission – see operation "Driveshaft, Remove and Install" in group F.

Seal ring from transmission housing – use MKM-557-1 (2) and MKM-557-2 (1).

Coat new seal ring with transmission fluid.

  Install

New seal ring – drive in with KM-519 (3) until flush with transmission housing.

Driveshaft to transmission.

  Inspect

Level of transmission fluid and correct if necessary – see operation "Transmission Fluid Level, Check and Correct".
