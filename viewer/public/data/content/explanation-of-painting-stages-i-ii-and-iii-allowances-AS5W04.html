Explanation of Painting Stages I, II and III Allowances

The time allowances for major and combination painting operations are divided into painting stages.

Paint stage

Description

I

Painting new components

 

o Top-coat complete surface

 

o Fill complete surface

 

o Partially apply filler on repaired parts

II

Painting surfaces

 

o Top-coat complete surface, including small improvements (without

 

filler application)

III

Repair

 

o Top-coat complete surface

 

o Fill working surface

 

o Partially apply putty to up to 50% of surface to be painted in

 

operation area.
