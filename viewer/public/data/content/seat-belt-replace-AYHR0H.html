Seat Belt, Replace

Instructions that follow are for left seat belt; right seat belt is similar.

  Remove

Soft top or hard top from vehicle – see Owner's Manual.

Seat belt from right seat – anchor bolt.

Right seat from floor – bolts.


Seat belt for right seat from roll bar – upper anchor bolt.


Seat belt from left seat – anchor bolt.

Left seat from floor – bolts.


Seat belt for left seat from roll bar – upper anchor bolt.


Loudspeakers from rear bulkhead trim – loudspeaker covers, 6 screws.

Rear bulkhead trim – 6 screws.

Soundproof insulation from rear bulkhead.


Harness connector from left seat belt tensioner.

Left inertia reel assembly from roll bar – lower anchor bolt.

  Install

Left inertia reel assembly to roll bar – lower anchor bolt.

Harness connector to left seat belt tensioner.


Soundproof insulation to rear bulkhead.

Rear bulkhead trim – 6 screws.

Loudspeakers to rear bulkhead trim – 6 screws, loudspeaker covers.


Seat belt for left seat to roll bar – upper anchor bolt.


Left seat to floor – bolts.

Seat belt to left seat – anchor bolt.


Seat belt for right seat to roll bar – upper anchor bolt.


Right seat to floor – bolts.

Seat belt to right seat – anchor bolt.

Soft top or hard top to vehicle – see Owner's Manual.
