Seats, Upholstery, Inner Trim Panels  

Seats  

The driver's seat is adjustable longitudinally.  

Support for the driver's back is provided by means of an inflatable lumbar support, which is installed in the backrest.  

A manually-operated pump (1) is installed for inflating the lumbar support. The lumbar support is deflated by depressing a pushbutton valve on the pump.  

The passenger's seat is non-adjustable.
