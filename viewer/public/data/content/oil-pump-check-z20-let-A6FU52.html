Oil Pump, Check (Z20 LET)

  Remove

1.   Remove oil pump, see operation “Oil Pump, Replace”
2.   Remove rear cover from oil pump
•   Remove 5x screw
•   Remove gears from oil pump body
•   Remove pressure regulating valve from oil pump body

  Clean

3.   Clean inner, outer gear and oil pump body
•   Check inner, outer gear and oil pump body for signs of wear
•   Clean, check pressure regulating valve for signs of wear

  Inspect

4.   Install oil pump gears to oil pump body
•   Position alignment marks (1)
Important:   If measurements are outside specified range, Install new pump.
5.   Check side measurement between gears and straight edge (1)
•   0.03 mm to 0.10 mm



  Install

6.   Install rear cover to oil pump
•   Install 5x screw 8 Nm
•   Install pressure regulating valve to oil pump body 50 Nm
– Use new sealing washer
7.   Remove crankshaft front seal from oil pump, then discard
•   Use suitable tool
– Clean crankshaft oil seal seat
8.   Install new crankshaft front seal to oil pump
•   Use Installer KM-693
•   Apply grease to seal
9.   Install oil pump, see operation “Oil Pump, Replace”
