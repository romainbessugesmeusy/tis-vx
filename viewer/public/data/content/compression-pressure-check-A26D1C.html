Compression Pressure, Check

Engine at operating temperature (minimum oil temperature 80 °C ).

  Remove

Direct ignition module – see operation "Direct Ignition Module, Remove and Install".

Use KM-194-E spark plugs.

Fuel pump relay from luggage compartment.

  Inspect

Use a compression pressure recorder with a rubber cone and a measuring range up to 40 bar .

Operate the starter for approx. 4 seconds with the throttle valve wide open – minimum engine speed 300 rpm .

Max. pressure difference between cylinders: 1 bar (14.7 psi) .

  Install

Fuel pump relay

Use KM-194-E , 25 Nm spark plugs.

Direct ignition module.
