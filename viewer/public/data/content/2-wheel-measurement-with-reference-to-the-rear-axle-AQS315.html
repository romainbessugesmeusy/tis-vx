2-wheel measurement with reference to the rear axle

With this measurement procedure, in addition to the normal 2-wheel-measurement, the axis of symmetry is included in the measurement. However, the front and rear axles have to be set separately from each other.

Important:   Alignment machines with 2-wheel alignment with reference to the rear axle are not approved for Opel/Vauxhall vehicles.

This measurement process includes e.g. visual-mechanical alignment machines.
