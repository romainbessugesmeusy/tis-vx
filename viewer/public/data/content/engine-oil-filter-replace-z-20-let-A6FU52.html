Engine Oil Filter, Replace (Z 20 LET)

  Remove

Important:   Observe relevant environmental regulations when disposing of used engine oil.

Remove oil filter with KM-726-A (1) - place collecting basin underneath.


  Install

Coat seal ring of new oil filter slightly with engine oil and attach to oil pump - tightening torque 15 Nm .

Top up engine with oil.

  • Run engine to prime oil filter.
  • Adjust oil level if necessary.

  Inspect

Check engine oil level and correct if necessary.
