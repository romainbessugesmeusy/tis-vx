Oil Cooler, Oil Lines, Replace (Z20 LET)

  Remove

Remove intake manifold – see operation "Intake Manifold, Replace (Z20 LET)".

Remove oil lines (1) from oil cooler – place collecting basin underneath.

Unclip oil lines from bracket (2).

Remove oil lines from thermostat housing (oil cooler).


  Install

Caution

Tighten thermostat housing (oil cooler) and oil lines evenly – Ensure that oil lines are not installed under tension.

Attach oil lines to thermostat housing for oil cooler, and oil cooler, using new seal rings – tightening torque 30 Nm .

Clip oil lines into bracket.

Install intake manifold – see operation "Intake Manifold, Replace (Z20 LET)".

  Inspect

Check engine oil level and correct as necessary.
