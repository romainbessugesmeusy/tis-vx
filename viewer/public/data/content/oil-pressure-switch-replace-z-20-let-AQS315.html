Oil Pressure Switch, Replace (Z 20 LET)

  Remove

Detach wiring harness plug (1) from oil pressure switch.

Disconnect oil pressure switch (2) from oil pump - place collection pan underneath.

  Install

Install oil pressure switch (2) with new seal ring to oil pump - tightening torque 40 Nm .

Connect wiring harness plug (1) to oil pressure switch.

  Inspect

Check engine oil level and correct if necessary.
