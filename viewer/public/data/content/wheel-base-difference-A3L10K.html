Wheel base difference

The wheel base difference is the angle (2) produced by the wheel offset (front and/or rear) between the contact lines (1,3) of the wheel contact points for the front and rear axles.

The angle is positive if the right wheel base greater than the left wheel base and negative if the left wheel base is greater than the right.

The wheel base difference is generally given in degrees. If the nominal wheel base value is known, length data can also be given.
