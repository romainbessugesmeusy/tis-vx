Clutch Master Cylinder, Remove and Install

  Remove

Hydraulic lines (arrowed) from master cylinder.

Pin from brake pedal and push rod of master cylinder – clip, washers.

Master cylinder from support bracket – nuts.


  Install

Master cylinder to support bracket – new nuts 20 Nm .

Pin to brake pedal and push rod of master cylinder – washers, clip.

Hydraulic lines (arrowed) to master cylinder.

Bleed clutch hydraulics – see operation "Hydraulic Clutch, Bleed".
