Brake Pads, Inspect for Wear

Illustration shows front and rear brake pads on left side, right side is similar.

  Remove

Wheels from vehicle – bolts.

  Measure

Thickness of brake pads (excluding backing).

Minimum brake pad thickness (excluding backing) – 2.5 mm .

Important:   All four brake pads on an axle must be replaced if any brake pad is less than minimum thickness.

  Install

Wheels to vehicle – bolts 90 Nm .
