Shock Absorber, Remove and Install

  Remove

Rear wheel from vehicle.

Rear wheel arch liner – screws, clips.

Shock absorber from lower wishbone – bolt, nut (1), washers.


  Remove

Shock absorber from bracket on chassis – bolt, nut, washers.

  Install

Shock absorber to bracket on chassis – bolt, nut 45 Nm , washers.


Shock absorber to lower wishbone – bolt, nut (1) 45 Nm , washers.

Rear wheel arch liner – screws, clips.

Rear wheel to vehicle – bolts 90 Nm .
