Wheel Bearing Assembly, Remove and Install  

  Remove, Disconnect   

Appropriate front wheel from vehicle.  

Brake caliper from hub carrier - two bolts; move to one side and support.  

Brake disc - one screw.  



 

 

  Remove, Disconnect   

Harness connector from wheel speed sensor (2).  

Bolt (3), nut, thrust washers and dust shield from wheel bearing assembly.  

Wheel bearing assembly from hub carrier - three bolts (1).  

  Install, Connect   

Wheel bearing assembly to hub carrier - bolts.  

Bolt, thrust washers and dust shield to wheel bearing assembly - 210 Nm.  

Harness connector to wheel speed sensor.  

Brake disc - one screw.  

Brake caliper to hub carrier - two bolts.
