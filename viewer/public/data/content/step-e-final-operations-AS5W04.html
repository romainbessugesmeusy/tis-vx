Step E - Final Operations

E1 - Assemble vehicle

  • If possible, perform step E2 before assembling the panelling.
  • Secure wiring harnesses at original location with original parts, e.g. corrugated tube, wiring trough brackets, cable ties, clips, etc.
  • Re-attach panelling and attaching parts.

E2 - Perform function check

  • Connect battery.
  • Perform function check. This ensures that the wiring harness repair has been performed successfully.

E3 - Program volatile memories

  • After completing all operations, reprogram volatile memories and clear audio equipment using code.
