Induction System - Speedster/VX220 Turbo

Induction air is drawn into the air cleaner via the air intake on the left side. The air cleaner is similar to that installed in the Astra Coupé, Turbo Edition.


The air is routed from the air cleaner to the turbocharger and then to the air-cooled intercooler, which is located behind the air intake on the right side of the vehicle.


The cooled induction air is routed from the intercooler to the inlet manifold of the engine.


•  Service:

Access to the air cleaner housing can be gained by removal of the left wheel arch liner.
