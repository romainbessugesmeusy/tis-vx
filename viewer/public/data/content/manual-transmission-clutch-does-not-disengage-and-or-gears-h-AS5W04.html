Field Remedy: 2130

Subject: Manual transmission - Clutch does not disengage and/or gears hard to engage
Models: Engines: Option:
All All
Complaint: Clutch does not disengage and /or gears hard to engage.
Cause: Toothing of the input shaft not greased (either forgotten in procution or after clutch replacement in service).
Production:

Remedy:

In case of customer complaint check first the greasing of the input shaft. 
If necessary thinly grease gear teeth of input shaft with grease 
(partnumber: 90397024 / cataloguenumber: 19 48 568). Please do 
not change any other parts.
FunctionalGroup: K - Clutch/Transmission
Complaint Group: 08 - Hard to operate
Trouble Code: None
