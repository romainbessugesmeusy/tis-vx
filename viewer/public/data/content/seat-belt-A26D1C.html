Seat Belts  

The driver and passenger are provided with three-point, inertia-reel seat belts. The automatic retractors are secured to the roll bar and incorporate pyrotechnic seat belt tensioners (see Section 4.4 "Airbag and Pyrotechnic Seat Belt Tensioner System").  

A load limiting mechanism is incorporated in the automatic retractor. This mechanism allows a controlled amount of relaxation to occur in the seat belt if the load on the seat belt exceeds a certain level. The illustration shows the seat belt of the right seat. The seat belt of the left seat is similar.
