Fuel and Exhaust Systems  

Fuel System  

Fuel Tank  

The steel fuel tank has a capacity of 39.8 litres.  

The fuel tank is mounted within the crossmember between the passenger compartment and the engine bay.  



A roll-over valve is located on the neck of the fuel filler. The valve allows venting of the fuel tank into an activated carbon canister, but prevents spillage of fuel if the vehicle is inverted in an accident.  



A locking, fuel filler cap is located behind the right door on left and right drive vehicles.
