Brake Fluid Reservoir  

The brake fluid reservoir is inserted in the top of the master cylinder. A fluid supply is also provided from the brake fluid reservoir to the clutch master cylinder.  

A float switch monitors the level of the brake fluid in the reservoir and is connected to a telltale in the instrument panel.
