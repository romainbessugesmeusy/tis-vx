Mass Air Flow Meter, Replace (Z 20 LET)

  Remove

1.   Disconnect battery negative lead
•   Release windscreen wash reservoir, then move to one side
•   Remove nut, bolt
2.   Disconnect harness connector (1) from mass air flow meter
3.   Disconnect engine breather hose (4) from air intake hose (3)
•   Release clip
4.   Remove air intake hose with mass air flow meter (2) from air cleaner housing and turbocharger
•   Release 2x clip



5.   Remove mass air flow meter from air intake hose
•   Release clip

  Install

6.   Install mass air flow meter to air intake hose
•   Secure clip
7.   Install air intake hose (3) with mass air flow meter (2) to air cleaner housing and turbocharger
•   Secure 2x clip
8.   Connect engine breather hose (4) to air intake hose
•   Secure clip
9.   Connect harness connector (1) to mass air flow meter



10.   Connect battery negative lead
•   Install nut, bolt
•   Secure windscreen wash reservoir
11.   Programme all volatile memories
