Subject: Engines (all) - Usage of engine oil additives
Models: Engines: Option:
All 2000... All
Complaint: Usage of engine oil additives
Cause:
Production:

Remedy:

Internal lab and engine test bench investigation revealed that the usage of not released oil additives will cause 
an increased engine oil consumption. In some cases the measured oil consumption has increased by 100%. 
Modern Opel engines in combination with high quality engine oil do not need additives. Therefore the 
usage of any not Opel released engine oil additive is disapproved. All related warranty claims will 
be rejected.
