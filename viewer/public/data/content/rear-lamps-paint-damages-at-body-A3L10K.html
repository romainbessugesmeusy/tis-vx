Field Remedy: 1769

Subject: Rear lamps - Paint damages at body
Models: Engines: Option:
Speedster 2000...2004,5,VX220 2000...2004,5 All,All
Complaint: Paint damages by contact with rear lamp units.
Cause: Body tolerances at manufactoring process.
Production: Improved rear lamp units in production since CW 1/2004.

Remedy:

 1. Remove rear lamps


    - see working procedure "Rear Lamp, Remove and Install",
    group "N", Service Instruction Speedster/VX220.
    (Document Number 00031700)


    Note:
    retain the selflocking nuts and washers to re-use later.


 2. Inspect body work for paint damages


    - repair possible paint damages per standard 
      paint repair process


 3. Apply commercially available transparent protective tape (1)
    on aperture surface of rear lamp.
    (Picture I)


    - clean surface with a commercial silicone remover
    - apply protective tape to give extra protection to painted 
      surface




 4. Carefully chamfer all lens edges of rear lamps.
    (Picture II)


    Notice:
    Use sanding block and sanding paper.




 5. Remove 3x top tips (arrows) completely at the back of each rear
    lamp. (Picture III)


    Important:
    Be careful not to damage the lens edges of the rear lamps.




 6. Add sealing strip S-10582 all around rear lamp edge.
    (hatched area in Picture IV)


    Note:
    Apply sealing strip 2 mm away from the outer circular angles.
    Sealing strip is necessary to avoid visible gap and contact
    to aperture surfaces.




 7. Attach 4x specified washers to studs (arrows) of each rear lamp.
    (Picture V)




 8. Align rear lamp in aperture. (Picture VI)


    - install lamp and check for constant gap condition
    - use a feeler gauge (1) 1 mm thickness to maintain
      a correct gap on bottom of lamp 


    Note:
    Circumferential gap should be 1-2 mm.
    Remove the lamp and correct the fit by adding or removing 
    specified washers if clearance is not acceptable. 


    Important:
    Act very carefully to avoid any damage to the painted surfaces 
    and body work.




 9. Install lamps if acceptable gap is achieved


    - see working procedure "Rear Lamp, Remove and Install",
      group "N", Service Instruction Speedster/VX220.
     (Document Number 00031700)
