Wash Reservoir, Remove and Install

  Remove

Harness connector from windscreen wash pump.

Lift screen wash reservoir from support bracket.

Hose from pump – have container available to collect wash fluid.

Pump from reservoir.

  Install

Pump to reservoir.

Hose to pump.

Screen wash reservoir to support bracket.

Harness connector to windscreen wash pump.

Refill screen wash reservoir with wash fluid.
