Front Door  

The front door pivots on a single hinge, which is secured to the door and to the chassis frame by means of bolts. The door side of the hinge includes an extruded aluminium beam, which functions as a side impact protector. A spring plate functions as a door restraint and a rubber-type strip limits the movement of the door when in the fully open position.

The alignment of the door is adjusted by the position of the hinge on the chassis frame and by the insertion of shims, which are located between the chassis frame and the hinge.  

Replacement bushes and bearings are available for the hinge.
