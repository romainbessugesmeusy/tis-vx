Weights – Speedster/VX 220

As of MY 1997, kerb weights and payloads will include the driver's weight in conformance with the new 70/156/EEC standard.

The front and rear axle loads together must not exceed the permissible total weight, i.e. if the front axle weight allowance is exceeded, the rear axle may only be loaded up to the limit of the total permissible weight.
