Bushings, Lower Wishbone, Replace

  Remove

Lower wishbone from rear suspension – see operation "Lower Wishbone, Remove and Install".

Bushings from lower wishbone – press out.

Important:   Front and rear bushings are different.

Coat new bushings with IPC 'P-80' rubber lubricant.


  Install

Bushings to lower wishbone – press in.

Lower wishbone to rear suspension.
