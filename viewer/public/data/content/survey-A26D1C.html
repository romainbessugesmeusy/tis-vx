R

The Technical Data Section is at the end of each main group; data relating to all groups is at the end of this introduction.

The part numbers listed in these Service Instructions are not available or permitted in all countries.

The contents of these Service Instructions are based on latest product information available at the time of publication. Questions which arise from reading the Service Instructions can be answered by General Motors Europe, Aftersales, Adam Opel AG, PKZ 66-06, D-65423 Rüsselsheim.

The service operations are described in a series of steps, which correspond to the Labour Time Guide publications in sequence, content and work scope. Operations for the various groups are described and shown primarily on vehicles with left hand drive. Procedures on vehicles with right hand drive are same. Where there are significant differences, this will be pointed out.

Special Service Tools are listed in the catalogue "Opel/Vauxhall Special Service Tools", other necessary tools, devices and technical equipment are listed in the catalogue "Opel/Vauxhall Workshop Equipment". In addition, the Opel special tools required for performing the described operations are listed at the end of each group, before the Technical Data Tables.
