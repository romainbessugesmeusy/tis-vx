Survey - Speedster/VX220 Turbo

The Speedster/VX220 Turbo is equipped as follows:

  • Turbocharged two litre engine as installed in Astra-G Coupé, Turbo Edition
  • F23 transmission as installed in Astra-G Coupé, Turbo Edition, with the same gear ratios
  • Revised rear clamshell which incorporates a modified engine cover
  • Re-designed side air intakes
  • Front and rear spoilers
  • Revised radiator grille for Opel vehicles
  • New wheel design
  • New speed rating for tyres
  • Modified lower wishbones for rear suspension
  • 45 Ah battery
  • New fuel level gauge
  • Optional footrest on passenger side, which is non-adjustable
  • Optional keyless entry/central door locking
