Field Remedy: 1716

Subject: Oil/Water Heat Exchanger - Leaky
Models: Engines: Option:
Alle Other (See options) Diesel and V6-engines
Complaint: Leakages/cracks on oil/water heat exchanger caused by corrosion due to coolant liquid which was not according to Opel specification.
Cause: Wrong coolant liquid and/or wrong mixture ratio.
Production:

Remedy:

When replacing the coolant liquid for maintanance or repair reasons, please
make sure that 
a.) the correct liquid is used, 
b.) the correct mixture ratio is assured. 
Correct coolant liquid and mixture ratio are included in TIS2000 Service
Instruction.
FunctionalGroup: J - Engine
Complaint Group: 01 - Broken or Cracked
Trouble Code: None
