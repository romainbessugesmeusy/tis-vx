Steering Arm, Remove and Install  

  Remove, Disconnect   

Tie rod from steering arm - nut; use suitable ball joint remover.  



  Remove, Disconnect   

Swivel joint from steering arm - nut; use suitable ball joint remover.  

Steering arm from hub carrier - bolts; retain camber shims.  



 

 

  Install, Connect   

Steering arm to hub carrier - shims, bolts 45 Nm.  

Swivel joint to steering arm - nut 45 Nm.  



  Install, Connect   

Tie rod to steering arm - nut 30 Nm.
