Engine Identification  

Four characters are used for identifying features of the engine.  

Z  

Exhaust emission limits, 98/69/EG, Euro 4, grade B.  

 

 

22  

Engine size, displacement in litres x 0.1.  

 

 

S  

Compression ratio, S = 9.5 - 10.0:1.  

 

 

E  

Carburation system, E = fuel injection.
