Thermostat, Replace (Z 20 LET)

Note:   The thermostat is only supplied in assembly with the thermostat housing for warranty reasons.

  Remove

Drain cooling system - collect escaping coolant.

Detach 2x coolant hose (1) from thermostat housing.

Remove thermostat housing (2) from cylinder head. Remove 2x bolt.


  Clean

Clean sealing surfaces and remove gasket residues.

  Install

Attach thermostat housing to cylinder head with new seal ring. Install 2x bolt 15 Nm .

Attach 2x coolant hose to thermostat housing.

Fill and bleed cooling system - see operation "Cooling System, Drain, Fill and Bleed" and "Cooling System, Check for Leaks".
