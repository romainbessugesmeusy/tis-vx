Clutch Plate and/or Pressure Plate, Remove and Install

  Remove

Transmission – see operation "Transmission, Remove and Install".

Lock flywheel with KM-652 (1), then release pressure plate and disc from flywheel – bolts (arrowed).

  Install

Pressure plate and disc to flywheel – use Clutch Aligning Guide KM-736 (2), locate pressure plate over centring pins. Use new bolts – 15 Nm .

Transmission.

Bleed clutch hydraulics – see operation "Hydraulic Clutch Bleed".
