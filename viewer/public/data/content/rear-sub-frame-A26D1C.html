Rear Sub-frame  

The rear sub-frame provides a mounting for the rear clamshell, the stays of the roll bar, the exhaust system, a torque reaction damping block for the engine, and the majority of the rear suspension.  

Tubular ducts in the rear sub-frame enable air from the engine compartment to pass over the exhaust system.  

The rear sub-frame is constructed from sheet steel and is secured to the rear of the chassis frame by means of four bolts.
