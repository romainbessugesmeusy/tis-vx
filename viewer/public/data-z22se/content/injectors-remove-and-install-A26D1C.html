Injectors, Remove and Install

  Remove

Engine wiring harness (2) from the fuel distribution pipe – cable ties.

Breather hose (1) from the cylinder head cover – clip.

Wiring harness connector (3) from the throttle valve housing.

Wiring harness connector from injectors.

Wiring harness connector (1) from the engine control unit.

Ground cable from the engine control unit – bolt (2).

  Remove

Fuel lines (arrow) from the fuel distribution pipe.

Important:   Fuel may escape – observe the safety regulations and relevant national legislation.

  Remove

Vacuum line (arrow) from the fuel pressure regulator.

Fuel distribution pipe from the intake manifold.

Injectors from the fuel distribution pipe.

  Install

Injectors into the fuel distribution pipe – use new seals.

Fuel distribution pipe to the intake manifold.

Vacuum line to the fuel pressure regulator.

Fuel lines (arrow) to the fuel distribution pipe.

Ground cable to the engine control unit – bolt (2).

Wiring harness connector (1) to the engine control unit.

Wiring harness connector to injectors.

Wiring harness connector (3) to the throttle valve housing.

Breather hose (1) to the cylinder head cover – clip.

Engine wiring harness (2) to the fuel distribution pipe – cable ties.
