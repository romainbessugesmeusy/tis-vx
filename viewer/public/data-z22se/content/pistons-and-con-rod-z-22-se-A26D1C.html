Pistons and Con-rod  

The pistons are made of cast aluminium.  

Each piston is equipped with two compression rings and a three-piece oil control ring. The piston rings are a low tension type to reduce friction. The top compression ring is ductile iron with molybdenum facing and phosphate coated sides. The second compression ring is grey iron. The oil control ring is chrome plated.  

The piston pin is fully-floating and is retained by means of two circlips.  

The con-rods are of sintered steel.
