Brake Pads, Front, Replace

  Remove

Wheel from vehicle – bolts.

Brake pads from brake caliper – retaining clips, pins (1 and 3), anti-rattle plate (2).

  Inspect

Caliper pistons – push caliper pistons fully into brake caliper.

If there is any sign of fluid leakage from a piston seal, replace brake caliper.


  Clean

Brake pad recess in brake caliper – avoid dust inhalation.

  Install

Brake pads to brake caliper – anti-rattle plate (2), pins (1 and 3), retaining clips.

Wheel to vehicle – bolts 90 Nm .

  Adjust

Caliper pistons and brake pads – operate brake pedal several times to reset.

  Inspect

Fluid level in master cylinder reservoir – correct if necessary.
