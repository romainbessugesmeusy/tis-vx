Manual Transmission F23  

General  

The Speedster/VX 220 is equipped with the F23 manual transmission, familiar from the Astra-G, Vectra-B and Zafira. The F23 has features as follows:  

 

•  High transmittable torque.  

•  Synchronisers on all five forward gears and reverse gear.  

•  Molybdenum coated synchroniser rings.  

•  Two-piece housing.  

 

• Service  

The transmission is lubricated for life.
