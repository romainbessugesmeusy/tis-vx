Step A - Observe Service Literature, Safety Regulations

A1 - Carefully read service literature

The following information is available:

  • Service instructions and the circuit diagrams and/or electrical systems manuals applicable for the model year are needed for performing wiring harness repair.
  • The information for the system introduction required for the repair can be found in section 5 "Parts and Reference Lists" .
  • Additional model versions as well as new applications will be published in stages in the context of the publication of circuit diagram information and in the EPC.
  • Product information video VT39
  • TIS 2000 CD39 for Astra-G and Zafira

Harness repair information is issued with the wiring diagram application. When for a connector a spare part is available and repair is allowed, the text “Connector view” in the drop down box will be changed into “Connector view and repair”. When with a component the text “Connector view and repair” is given, the connector code (1) and the correct special tools (2) are given with the connector view.
