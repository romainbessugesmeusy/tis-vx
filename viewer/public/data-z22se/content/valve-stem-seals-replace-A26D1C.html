Valve stem seals, replace

Special service tools required:

Push-in tool KM-352, KM-663, KM-835-A, KM-958, KM-6385, extraction pliers KM-840

Note:   All attaching parts such as camshaft(s) and hydraulic valve lifters are removed.

  Install

Attach side mounts KM-6215-5 (1) for lever KM-6215-4 (2) to assembly device. Attach lever KM-6215-4 (2).

See operation "Cylinder head, disassemble" , "Cylinder head, assemble" .
