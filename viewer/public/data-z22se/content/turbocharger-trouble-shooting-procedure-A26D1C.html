Field Remedy: 2483

Subject: Turbocharger - Trouble-Shooting Procedure
Models: Engines: Option:
Alle - Turbo Charger
Complaint: Low performance, Noisy, Leaky, Black Smoke
Cause: Many turbochargers are being replaced unnecessarily because a trouble-shooting procedure is not being followed.
Production: None.

Remedy:

Most turbocharger failures are caused by one of the three basic
reasons:


     • Lack of Lubrication
     • Oil Contamination
     • Foreign Object Damage

Turbocharger parts overview:

1. Turbin housing
2. Thrust bearing
3. Compressor wheel
4. REA (Elektronic Actuator)
5. Compressor housing
6. Journal bearing
7. Nozzle ring assembly (turbine wheel)





Many turbochargers are replaced needlessly because a trouble-shooting 
procedure is not being followed.
