Body Measurement Guide

The traditional fast measurement method using a telescopic rule MKM-642-A is not permitted.

Further procedure:

Visually inspect the aluminium chassis for damage.

If chassis damage cannot be definitely ruled out, then the vehicle dimensions must be measured.

Measure the vehicle dimensions using the usual straightening system and its data sheets.

Keep a record of the measurements for the specialists and the insurance companies.

Where the body measurements are outside the tolerances given, take appropriate action.

Refer to the chapter 'Body Repair Using the Straightening System'.
