Switches  

The combination switches on the steering column are carried over from the Corsa-B.  

 
A bank of switches is located in the instrument panel.  



  

 

13.6   Switches (continued)  

A pushbutton starter switch (1) is located in the centre of the instrument panel.  

This switch is disabled via a relay, once the engine is running, to prevent subsequent engagement of the starter.  



A pushbutton switch (1) is located in the steering column cowl to reset the odometer to zero and to control the brightness of the instrument illumination.
