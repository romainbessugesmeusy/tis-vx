DIS Ignition Module Control Unit, Replace

1.   Open the bonnet.
2.   Disconnect the battery.
3.   Open the rear engine cover.
4.   Detach the ignition module control unit.
•   Release and detach the wiring harness connector.
•   3 bolts (1)
5.   Attach the ignition module control unit.
•   Coat the bolts with thread locking compound (red) and insert them.
•   3 bolts
– Tightening torque 8 Nm
•   Attach and secure the wiring harness connector.
6.   Close the rear engine cover.
7.   Connect the battery.
8.   Close the bonnet.
9.   Reprogram the volatile memories.
