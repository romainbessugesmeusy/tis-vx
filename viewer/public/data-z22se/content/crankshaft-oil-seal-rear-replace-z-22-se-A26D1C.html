Crankshaft Oil Seal, Rear, Replace (Z 22 SE)

1.   Remove the transmission.
•   Note:
See operation "Transmission, Remove and Install" in Group K
2.   Detach the drive plate.
•   Immobilise the drive plate via the starter ring gear with KM-652 (1).
•   6 bolts



3.   Remove the crankshaft rear oil seal (1).
•   Use a suitable tool to prise it out – do not damage the sealing surfaces.
4.   Install the crankshaft rear oil seal.
•   Clean the sealing surfaces.
•   Coat the sealing lip with silicone grease (white).
•   Turn back the installation tool KM-J-42067 (2).
•   Slide the oil seal onto KM-J-42067 .
•   Attach KM-J-42067 to the crankshaft.
•   Press in the oil seal with KM-J-4206 7 until KM-J-42067 lies flush against the housing.
•   Detach KM-J-42067 .
5.   Attach the drive plate.
•   Recut the thread in the crankshaft.
•   Use new bolts.
•   Tightening torque 53 Nm + 25°
•   Detach KM-652 .
6.   Install the transmission.
•   Note:
See operation "Transmission, Remove and Install" in Group K
