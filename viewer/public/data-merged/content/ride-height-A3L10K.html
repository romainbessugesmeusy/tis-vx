Ride height

Important:   For the rear axle of the Vectra-C there are several settings which relate to the different vehicle ride heights. The ride height given (l) is measured form the centre of the wheel hub to the lower edge of the wheel arch on each vehicle side. Any slope of the vehicle caused by different ride heights on the left and right rear axle must be compensated. The vehicle must stand horizontal at the rear by dividing the weights in the rear onto the higher vehicle side. The height difference between left and right vehicle side must not exceed 1 mm .
