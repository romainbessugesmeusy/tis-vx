Throttle Body, Replace (Z20 LET)

  Remove

Disconnect ground cable from battery.

Drain coolant – see operation ”Coolant System, Drain, Fill and Bleed”

Remove intercooler hose (5) from throttle body, upper.

Remove throttle body, upper (4) from throttle body, lower.

Remove throttle body coolant lines (2) from throttle body. Remove engine vent hose (3) from cylinder head cover.

Disconnect throttle body wiring harness plug (1).

Remove throttle body.


  Install

Replace throttle body sealing rings and attach throttle body coolant lines to throttle body. Attach engine vent hose to cylinder head cover.

Attach throttle body, upper to throttle body, lower – tightening torque 9 Nm . Attach intercooler hose to throttle body upper – tightening torque – 3.5 Nm .

Connect ground cable to battery.

Charge cooling system – see operations ”Coolant System, Drain, Fill and Bleed”.

Perform a relearning procedure after throttle body module (E-Gas) replacement. To do so, turn on ignition and wait at least 10 seconds. Under no circumstances should starting occur during the waiting period as this would interrupt the relearning procedure.
