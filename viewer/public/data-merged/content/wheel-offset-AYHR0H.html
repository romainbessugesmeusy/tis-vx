Wheel offset

Wheel offset is the angular deviation between the connecting line (1) of the wheel contact points of an axle and a perpendicular (2) to the axis of symmetry (3) through one of the wheel contact points (4).

The angle is negative if the right wheel is to the front of the left wheel and positive if the left wheel is in front of the right wheel.

The wheel displacement is generally given in degrees. If the track width is known, length data can also be given.
