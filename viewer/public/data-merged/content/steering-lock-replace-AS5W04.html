Steering Lock, Replace

  Remove

Upper and lower shrouds from steering column – screws.

Lock cylinder from lock casing by inserting ignition key in ignition lock, then pressing down spring catch with the lock cylinder in position 1 (use wire or small screwdriver).


  Install

Press down spring catch with lock cylinder in position 1 using wire or small screwdriver. Insert lock cylinder and slide in until fully engaged.

Steering column upper and lower shrouds – screws.
