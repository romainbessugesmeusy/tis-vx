Fuel Pressure, Check (Z 20 LET)

  Inspect

Warning:   Fuel escapes. Observe safety regulations and national legislation.
1.   Connect Pressure Tester KM-J-34730-91 (1) to pressure relief valve (2) on fuel rail
•   Remove cap from pressure relief valve



2.   Start engine
3.   Bleed Pressure Tester KM-J-34730-91
•   Position suitable container to collect escaping fuel
•   Check fuel pressure
4.   Install Pressure and Vacuum Pump MKM-667 (2) to fuel pressure regulator (3)
•   Disconnect vacuum pipe from fuel pressure regulator
5.   Apply 0.5 bar using Pressure and Vacuum Pump MKM-667
6.   Check fuel pressure using Pressure Tester KM-J-34730-91 (1)



7.   Turn off engine
8.   Remove Pressure Tester KM-J-34730-91 (1)
•   Bleed excess fuel from Pressure Tester
– Position suitable container to collect escaping fuel
•   Disconnect Pressure Tester from pressure relief valve on fuel rail
– Install cap to pressure relief valve
9.   Remove Pressure and Vacuum Pump MKM-667 (2)
•   Connect vacuum pipe to fuel pressure regulator (3)
