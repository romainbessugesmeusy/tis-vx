Field Remedy: 1768

Subject: Instrument panel - Lifting at sides
Models: Engines: Option:
Speedster 2001...2003,VX220 2001...2003 All,All None.
Complaint: Instrument panel is lifting at the outer edges near the A-pillars.
Cause: The instrument panel becomes loose.
Production: As of VIN W0L0EBR973N007335 (LHD) and W0L0EBR973N007265 (RHD) the instrument panel is mounted using additional brackets.

Remedy:

In case of customer complaint fit additional brackets to the driver’s side
and passengers side.

Working procedure:

 1. Remove instrument panel



     - see working procedure "Instrument Pad Assembly, Remove and Install",
       Group "C", Service Instruction Speedster/VX220
       (Document-Number 00031508).



    Note:
    The heater control unit must not be removed.



 2. Lay instrument panel onto work bench.



    Important:
    Lay protection cloth under instrument panel.



 3. Remove identification plate with production number from instrument panel.
 4. Degrease brackets to provide better adhesion (Picture I, arrows).






 5. Degrease instrument panel left and right in hatched area (Picture II).






 6. Prepare 2K-adhesive.



    Note:
    - mix adhesive with hardener in a ratio of 1:1
    - pay attention to instructions on the rear of the packaging



 7. Install brackets to the left and the right side of the instrument panel
    (Picture III, 1).



    Note:
    - Measure "x" = 3 mm
    - fill-out the holes in brackets (arrowed) using the rest of 2K-adhesive
    - let the 2K-adhesive cure completely
    - pay attention to instructions on the rear of packaging






 8. Carefully remove adhesive strips of left and right cover
    (Picture IV, 1 and 2).






 9. Loosely refit instrument panel to vehicle.



    Important:
    Pay attention for correct position of instrument panel.



10. On the driver’s side mark the position of the bonded bracket onto the
    dash extrusion (Picture V, 1).



    Picture V shows passengers side on LHD vehicle.






11. On the driver’s side drill hole for bracket onto the dash extrusion with a
    3,5 mm drill bit.



    Note:
    Drill hole 2 mm above the marking, otherwise the light switch can not be
    reinstalled.



12. Install bracket with a blind rivet to dash extrusion (Picture VI).



    Picture VI shows passengers side on LHD vehicle.






13. On the passengers side mark the position of the bonded bracket onto the
    dash extrusion (Picture VII, 1).



    Note:
    - align position of bracket to bonded bracket
    - Measure "x" = 4 mm



    Picture VII shows passengers side on LHD vehicle.






14. On the passengers side drill hole for bracket onto the dash extrusion
    with a 3,5 mm drill bit.
15. Install bracket with a blind rivet to dash extrusion.



16. Drill through both the dash extrusion and the bonded bracket using a 3 mm
    drill bit (Picture VIII).



    Important:
    Extreme care must be taken when drilling to ensure the instrument panel
    is not damaged.



    Note:
    - ensuring first that the instrument panel is pushed down whilst drilling
    - also ensuring that the hole will penetrate into the centre of the
      bonded bracket



    Picture VIII shows driver’s side on LHD vehicle.






17. Remove instrument panel.
18. Drill a 5 mm clearance hole through the pre-drilled 3 mm hole in the
    driver’s side dash extrusion, which will aid final assembly.



    Note:
    Ensure any swarf is vacuumed out from inside the extrusion area.



19. Align instrument panel to the original mounting points.



    Note:
    Connect the starter switch.



20. Fasten instrument panel left and right (Picture IX, 1 and 2).






21. Install identification plate.
22. Clean working area.
23. Install instrument panel



     - see working procedure "Instrument Pad Assembly, Remove and Install",
       Group "C", Service Instruction Speedster/VX220
       (Document-Number 00031508).
