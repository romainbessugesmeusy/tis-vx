Field Remedy: 1832

Subject: Diesel Engines - Usage of Gasoline instead of Diesel Fuel
Models: Engines: Option:
All Diesel Engines Diesel Engine
Complaint: The following failure modes can be detected (for example): bad warm start, engine destroyed, high pressure pump blocked; tooth belt torn; valves damaged
Cause: Due to the use of gasoline the high pressure pump gets destroyed (lack of lubrication) and the tooth belt will be torn. This may end up in the mentioned damages.
Production:

Remedy:

If the described failure is detected the usage of correct fuel has to 
be verified. A diesel fuel and engine oil sample has to be analyses 
for gas contamination. The needed test-bottles for the samples can be 
requested under special part-no. S-10571 (test-bottle 500ml).


Note:
Special parts can be ordered via e-mail-address
"etac-special-parts@de.gm.com".
Please add the VIN of the affected vehicle.


In case of gasoline refuelling on vehicles with diesel engine 
the whole fuel system has to be drained and rinsed. 


Note:
Warranty can only be claimed, if no usage of gasoline has been 
detected.
