Foreword

The brochures "Chassis, Wheel Suspension" and "Chassis Adjustment Values" contain all important information for carrying out chassis alignment for Opel/Vauxhall vehicles.

The present brochure "Chassis, Wheel Suspension" not only gives the trained but also the less experienced technician information on the fundamental concepts, nature and function of the various chassis alignment equipment and its use.

This brochure also contains all the current adjustment instructions for chassis alignment including the associated illustrations for all Opel/Vauxhall vehicles as of Model Year '99.

The brochure "Suspension Settings" contains the settings for the front and rear axles of all Opel/Vauxhall vehicles. These apply as of model year 99 and are updated for each model year and published as specific brochures for the model year. After publication, the brochure "Suspension Settings" for the model year concerned is not revised further.
