Oil Pressure, Check (Z 20 LET)

  Remove

Disconnect wiring harness plug (1) from oil pressure switch, remove oil pressure switch (2) from oil pump - place collecting basin underneath.

  Inspect

Check oil pressure with KM-498-B (3) and KM-135 (4). At idling speed, the oil pressure should be approx. 1.5 bar and the oil temperature should be ≥ 80 °C.


  Install

Install oil pressure switch with new seal ring to oil pump – tightening torque 40 Nm .

Connect wiring harness plug to oil pressure switch.

Check engine oil level and correct if necessary.
