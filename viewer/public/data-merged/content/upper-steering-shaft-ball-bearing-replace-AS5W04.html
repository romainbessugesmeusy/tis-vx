Upper Steering Shaft Ball Bearing, Replace

  Remove

Steering column – see operation "Steering Column, Remove and Install".

Switch casing from steering column – bolts (1).


  Disassemble

Spacer ring (1) from steering shaft (2).


Support steering column in vice.

  Disassemble

Centre punch bolts of steering and ignition lock casing.

Using 5 mm drill, spot drill shear bolts approx. 15 mm deep.

Drive screwdriver into bore and unscrew shear bolt.


  Disassemble

Centre punch 4 bolts of upper steering shaft housing.

Using 5 mm drill, spot drill shear bolts approx. 15 mm deep.

Drive screwdriver into bore and unscrew shear bolt.


  Disassemble

Upper housing with steering shaft ball bearing from steering column (illustration I).

Upper steering shaft ball bearing from housing using Remover/Installer KM-108 (illustration II).

  Assemble

Upper steering shaft ball bearing into housing using Remover/Installer KM-108 (illustration III).


Upper housing with steering shaft ball bearing to steering column – use new shear bolts. Tighten bolts until bolt head shears off.

Spacer ring (1) to steering shaft (2).


  Install

Switch casing to steering column – bolts (1).

Steering column – see operation "Steering Column, Remove and Install".
