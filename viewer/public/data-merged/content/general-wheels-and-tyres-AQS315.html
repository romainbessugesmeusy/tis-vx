Wheels and Tyres  

General  

The rims of all models are of magnesium and are secured to the hubs by means of five bolts. Security bolts are installed as standard equipment.  

The front wheels are 5.5 J x 17 and are inset 26 mm. The rear wheels are 7.5 J x 17 and are inset 39 mm.  



Tyre sizes are as follows:  

 

•  Front tyres are 175/55 R 17.  

•  Rear tyres are 225/45 R 17.  

 

• Service  

The tyres are directional and the sidewalls are marked with the direction of rotation.  

The changing of tyres between axles is not permitted.
