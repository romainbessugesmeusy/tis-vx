Ribbed V-belt Tension, Check (Z 20 LET)

  Inspect

Ribbed V-belt tension is adjusted via automatic ribbed V-belt tensioner.

The position of the movable tensioning arm (1) for the ribbed V-belt tensioner can be checked. This should be located between the stops (2).

If movable tensioner arm (1) of ribbed V-belt tensioner is located at one of the stops (2), replace ribbed V-belt and ribbed V-belt tensioner - see operations "Ribbed V-belt, Replace” and "Ribbed V-belt Tensioner, Replace”.
