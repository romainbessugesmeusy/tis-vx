Valve Stem Wear, Measure (Z 20 LET)

Special service tools required:

Caliper gauge, micrometer screw

  Remove

Remove all valves and clean.

Ensure that there are no crater-like burns on the valve cone.

  Measure

Determine the valve stem diameter using a micrometer screw. Valve length is measured using a caliper gauge.

  Inspect

Check valves for true running, lateral slack and contact pattern. Valve length, valve stem diameter, valve head diameters and valve seat widths - permissible dimensions can be found in the "Technical Data".
