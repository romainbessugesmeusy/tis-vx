Air Vents, Remove and Install

  Remove

Instrument pad assembly from vehicle – see operation "Instrument Pad Assembly, Remove and Install" in group C.

Air duct (1) from instrument pad assembly – screws.

Air vents from instrument pad assembly – pull out in direction of arrow from under instrument pad assembly to release from clips.

  Install

Air vents to instrument pad assembly – push in to engage in clips.

Air duct (1) to instrument pad assembly – screws.

Instrument pad assembly to vehicle – see operation "Instrument Pad Assembly, Remove and Install" in group C.
