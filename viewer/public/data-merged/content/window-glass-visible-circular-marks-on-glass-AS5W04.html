Field Remedy: 1863

Subject: Window Glass - Visible Circular Marks on Glass
Models: Engines: Option:
All All All
Complaint: Circular marks may 'appear' when the glass is damp.
Cause: Most production lines use rubber suction heads that can leave impression marks on the glass surface.
Production:

Remedy:

In case of customer complaint the glass should be polished using a suitable proprietary mild abrasive. Substances like jewellers rouge, certain teeth whitening products or toothpaste are particularly suitable.

FunctionalGroup: C - Equipment/Windows
Complaint Group: 07 - Foreign Material or Fluid
Trouble Code: None
