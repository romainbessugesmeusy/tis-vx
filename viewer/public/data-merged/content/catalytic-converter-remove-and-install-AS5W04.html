Catalytic Converter, Remove and Install

Open engine cover.

  Remove

Harness connectors (1) for oxygen sensors.


  Install

Chocks to front wheels.

Raise vehicle.

  Remove

Rear undertray (1) – 13 bolts.

Diffuser panel (2) – 7 bolts, 2 clips.


Wiring harness from rear subframe – clip.

Wiring harness from heatshield – cable ties, clip.

Wiring harness from wheelarch liner.

Lower heatshield (1) from exhaust manifold – 3 bolts, clip.


Catalytic converter from intermediate pipe – 3 nuts (1).


Catalytic converter from exhaust manifold – 3 nuts (1).

Catalytic converter from mounting (2).

  Clean

Gasket faces.

  Install

Catalytic converter to exhaust manifold – 3 nuts (1).

Catalytic converter to mounting (2).


Catalytic converter to intermediate pipe – 3 nuts (1).


Lower heatshield (1) to exhaust manifold – 3 bolts, clip.


Wiring harness through wheelarch liner.

Wiring harness to rear subframe – clip.

Wiring harness to heatshield – cable ties, clip.

  Install

Diffuser panel (2) – 7 bolts, 2 clips.

Important:   Do not tighten bolts.

  Install

Rear undertray (1) – 13 bolts.

Tighten diffuser and undertray bolts.


Lower vehicle.

  Install

Harness connectors (1) for oxygen sensors.

Close engine cover.

  Remove

Chocks from front wheels.
