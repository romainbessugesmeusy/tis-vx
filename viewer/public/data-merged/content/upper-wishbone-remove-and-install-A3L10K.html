Upper Wishbone, Remove and Install  

  Remove, Disconnect   

Clamp for brake hose (1) from upper wishbone - nut.  

Upper swivel joint (2) from steering arm - nut, use suitable ball joint remover.  



  Remove, Disconnect   

Upper wishbone from chassis - bolts, nuts, washers, shims; steering rack to be on full lock to provide sufficient clearance for removing rear bolt.  

  Important!   

Note distribution of shims for caster adjustment.  

  Install, Connect   

Upper wishbone to chassis - bolts, nuts 45 Nm, washers.  



 

 

  Install, Connect   

Upper swivel joint (2) to steering arm - nut 45 Nm.  

Clamp (1) for brake hose to upper wishbone - nut.
