Con-rod Bearing, Replace (Z20 LET)

  Remove

Remove upper part of oil pan – see operation "Oil Pan, Upper, Replace".

Remove balancer shaft unit – see operation "Balancer Shafts, Replace".

Mark order of con-rod bearing caps (1) – bead (2) of con-rod bearing caps points to transmission side.

Remove con-rod bearing cap from con-rod. Press con-rod bearing out of con-rod and con-rod bearing cap.


  Clean

Clean con-rod bearing journal and con-rod bearing cap.

  Install

Insert new con-rod bearing shells with engine oil. Attach con-rod bearing caps to con-rod with new bolts – tightening torque 35 Nm + 45° + 15° .

Install balancer shaft unit – see operation "Balancer Shafts, Replace".

Install upper part of oil pan – see operation "Oil Pan, Upper, Replace".
