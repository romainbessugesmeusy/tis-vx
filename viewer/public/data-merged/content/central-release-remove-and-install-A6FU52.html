Central Release, Remove and Install

  Remove

Transmission – see operation "Transmission, Remove and Install".

Pressure line (1) from central release.

Central release from clutch housing – bolts (arrowed).

  Inspect

Seal ring on central release for damage.

Note: central release and release bearing is one part. Overhauling is not possible. If damaged or leaking replace entire part.

Coat seal rings in central release with transmission fluid.

Central release to transmission housing – bolts (arrowed) 5 Nm .

Pressure line (1) to central release – 14 Nm .
