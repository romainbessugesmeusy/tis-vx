Instrument Pad Assembly, Remove and Install

  Remove

Facial panels (1 and 3) from instrument panel – carefully lever off.

Radio (4) and installation frame from instrument panel – see operation "Installation Frame, Radio, Remove and Install" in group R.

Control unit (5) from instrument panel – see operation "Control Unit, Remove and Install" in group D.

Instrument housing (2) and cover assembly from instrument panel – see operation "Instrument Housing, Remove and Install".

Switch assembly (6) from instrument panel – press clips to release, then move switch assembly to one side.


Instrument pad assembly from vehicle – screws; harness connector from starter button.

  Install

Instrument pad assembly to vehicle – harness connector to starter button; screws.


Switch assembly (6) to instrument panel – push in to engage clips.

Instrument housing (2) and cover assembly to instrument panel – see operation "Instrument Housing, Remove and Install".

Control unit (5) to instrument panel – see operation "Control Unit, Remove and Install" in group D.

Installation frame and radio (4) to instrument panel – see operation "Installation Frame, Radio, Remove and Install" in group R.

Facia panels (1 and 3) to instrument panel.
