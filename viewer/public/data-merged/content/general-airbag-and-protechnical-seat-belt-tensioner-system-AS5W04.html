Airbag and Pyrotechnic Seat Belt Tensioner System  

General  

Pyrotechnic safety systems are installed as standard and consist of:  

 

•  Driver's airbag.  

•  Pyrotechnic seat belt tensioners.  

 

The passenger's side of the compartment allows sufficient space for movement of the passenger in a front end collision.  

The driver's airbag and the seat belt tensioners are deployed in the event of a front end collision.  

Important: 

The safety regulations regarding the handling and disposal of pyrotechnic units (airbag and seat belt tensioners) must be strictly observed.  

• Service  

The pyrotechnic safety system is checked by means of TECH 2.
