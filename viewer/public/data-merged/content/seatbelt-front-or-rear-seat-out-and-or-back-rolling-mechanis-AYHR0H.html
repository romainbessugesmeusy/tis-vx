Field Remedy: 2792

Subject: Seatbelt front or rear seat - Out and/or back rolling mechanism does not work correctly
Models: Engines: Option:
All All
Complaint: Seatbelt does not roll back, or seatbelt rolls back too slowly, or seatbelt difficult to pull out.
Cause: Clothes fibres, dirt, dust or twisted belt webbing can cause seizing or blocking of the seatbelt. The seatbelt itself is ok. The seat belt guiding system needs to be checked.
Production:

Remedy:

In Case of customer complaint, check D-loop (deflect mechanism) 
for contamination regarding dirt, dust or webbing fibres.

Clean D-loop and seatbelt. Check function again by maximum pull out.

Sealbelt webbing must not be twisted! If still an issue, demount 
trim cover and check again.

If the seatbelt rolls back, no part must be replaced.

Reassemble Trim Cover and check function again. Take care of correct 
positioning of Trim Cover. No bending should occur, no sharp edges 
may have contact to the belt webbing! 


Caution: 
seat belt webbing should only be cleansed with warm water or suitable 
low concentration commercially available products. Strong solvent 
based products should not be used.
FunctionalGroup: C - Equipment/Windows
Complaint Group: 02 - Chafing,08 - Hard to operate,13 - No/Poor Function
