    G1 Battery
    Wiring harness connector (wiring harness side) terminal 31
  • Disconnect wiring harness connector from:
    B48 Power Sounder - Anti Theft Warning Unit
  • Connect wiring harness connector to:

  • Ignition ON
  • Measure voltage between the following terminals:
    B48 Power Sounder - Anti Theft Warning Unit
    Wiring harness connector (wiring harness side) terminal 4
    &
    Ground

Note:

To avoid a Power Sounder activation, disconnect ground cable from battery within 15s after switching off ignition.
