Towing Eye Bracket, Remove and Install

  Remove

Front clamshell – see operation "Front Clamshell, Remove and Install".

Towing eye bracket (1) – 3 screws (arrowed).

Important:  

Align the towing eye bracket in such a way that the longer side of the threaded bushing (2) is pointing in the direction of travel – secure screws in place with filler.

  Install

Towing eye bracket (1) – 3 screws (arrowed) 25 Nm .

Front clamshell – see operation "Front Clamshell, Remove and Install".
