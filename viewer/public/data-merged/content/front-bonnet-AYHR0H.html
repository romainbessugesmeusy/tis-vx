Glassfibre-composite Structures (Bolted)  

Front Bonnet  

The front bonnet is attached to the body by means of a single hinge at the front edge. The bonnet lock is operated by means of a cable.  

Two grilles are installed in the front bonnet for the passage of air from the radiator (see Section 2.5.1 "Radiator Grilles").
