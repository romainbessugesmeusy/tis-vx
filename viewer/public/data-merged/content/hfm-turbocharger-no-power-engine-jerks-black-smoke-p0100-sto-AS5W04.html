Field Remedy: 1607

Subject: HFM, Turbocharger - No power, engine jerks, black smoke, P0100 stored in ECU
Models: Engines: Option:
Speedster 2003,VX220 2003 Z20LET,Z20LET None
Complaint: No power, engine jerks, black smoke, P0100 stored in ECU.
Cause: Turbo charger hose loose at turbochareger side -> engine looses boost.
Production: Improved installation process as of VIN W0L0EBR973N006484 .

Remedy:

In case of customer complaint check the correct connection of hose between
turbocharger and intercooler. 
The jubilee clip at turbocharger side has to be tightened with 4 Nm.

Note: Replacement of HFM does not solve the problem.

FunctionalGroup: J - Engine
Complaint Group: 06 - Failure Code Setting by Control Unit
Trouble Code: P0100
