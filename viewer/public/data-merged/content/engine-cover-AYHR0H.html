Engine Cover  

The engine cover is attached to the body by means of a hinge at the front edge. The engine cover lock is operated by means of a cable.  

The engine cover is equipped with two grilles for the passage of air from the engine compartment (see Section 2.5.2 "Rear Grilles, Engine Compartment").
