6.2   Front Wheel Alignment (continued)  

• Service  

Camber is adjusted by means of shims (3), which are installed between the hub carrier and the steering arm.  

Caster is adjusted by means of shims (1 and 2), which are installed at the pivot points of the upper control arm. The transfer of a shim, or shims, from one side of a bush to the other side must be performed at both the front bush and the rear bush.

Toe-in is adjusted at the tie rods.
