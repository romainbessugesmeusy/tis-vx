Disposal

For disposal purposes, the waste products of paints, hardeners, thinners and additives should be separated according to their different properties. Plastic parts should also be separated out for disposal purposes (see corresponding instructions in the introduction).

Example:

  • Lacquer and paint sludge.
  • Waste lacquers, waste paint, not hardened.
  • Sludge containing solvents but no halogenated organic solvents.
  • Solvent mixes not containing halogenated organic solvents.
  • Plastic parts.

Please note that disposal must be carried out in compliance with relevant national legislation.
