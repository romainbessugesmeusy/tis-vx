Explanation of Pictograms

Pictograms are used in the following Service Instructions to provide a clear and comprehensible presentation of the various operations.

Pictograms and their meaning:

  Remove

Any type of removal from vehicle (unscrew, drive out, etc.).

  Install

Any type of installation on vehicle (bolt on, fit, etc.).

  Disassemble

Disassemble component for cleaning, checking, etc.

  Assemble

Assemble component after completing operational check.

  Clean

To ensure correct operation or decrease wear (bearing or sealing surfaces, etc.).

  Inspect

Check component for correct operation and wear. Check fluid level and correct if necessary.

  Measure

Check that components, temperature, pressure, etc. conform to the specified values.

  Adjust

All adjusting work according to specifications.

Torque

Tighten bolts or nuts to specified torque.

Torqangle

Tighten bolts or nuts to specified torque and turn further by angle.

Important:   Safety information or important explanation.

Caution

Processing of components and units.
