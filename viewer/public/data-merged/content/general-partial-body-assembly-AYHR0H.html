Partial Body Assembly  

General  

The partial body assembly consists of the following:  

 

•  Side panels (see Section 2.3.1 "Side Panel").  

•  Windscreen frame (see Section 2.3.2 "Windscreen Frame").  

•  Crash box assembly (see Section 2.3.3 "Crash Box Assembly, Front").  

•  Rear bulkhead (see Section 2.3.4 "Rear Bulkhead").  

•  Roll bar (see Section 2.6 "Roll Bar").  

•  Chassis frame (see Section 2.7.2 "Chassis Frame").  

 

 


 

 

 

 

• Service  

The partial body assembly is supplied with the radiator pipes, heater pipes, battery cable and clutch pipe.
