Battery / Starter (Voltage Measurement)

 

Objective:

Evaluation of the state of charge of the battery, voltage supply to the starter during starting and operation of the starter.

Measurement:

Plot of the battery voltage during the start procedure.

Preparation:

Switch off the engine, switch off the ignition, switch off all electrical loads.

Connections:

INPUT A

COM/TRIGGER

INPUT B

Connect the red measurement lead to the positive pole of the battery. Connect the black measurement lead to the negative pole of the battery.

1. Download Reference curve.
2. Start engine.
