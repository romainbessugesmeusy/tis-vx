Fuel Lines, Replace (Z20 LET)

  Remove

Important:   Fuel may escape – observe the safety regulations and relevant national legislation.
1.   Release fuel pressure from fuel system
•   Remove protective cap
•   Release fuel pressure using Pressure Tester KM-J-34730-91 (1)
•   Connect test connection hose (2) to fuel rail
– Collect any escaping fuel in a suitable container
2.   Remove Pressure Tester KM-J-34730-91 (1)
•   Disconnect test connection hose (2) from fuel rail
•   Install protective cap



3.   Disconnect 2x fuel line from fuel rail
Note:   Counter hold adapter with open end of spanner.
•   Install 4x blanking plug



4.   Disconnect 4x fuel lines (1)
•   Disconnect 2x quick release connector
– Use Remover KM-796-A
•   Install 4x blanking plug



5.   Disconnect 2x fuel line from fuel filter (1)
Note:   Counterhold fuel filter at hexagon with open end of spanner.
•   Install 4x blanking plug



6.   Release fuel tank from vehicle chassis – see operation “Fuel Tank, Remove and Install”
Note:   Lower fuel tank to enable access to fuel lines.
7.   Disconnect 2x fuel line (1) from fuel tank
•   Disconnect 2x quick release connector
– Use Remover KM-796-A
•   Install 4x blanking plug



  Install

8.   Connect 2x fuel line to fuel tank
•   Remove 4x blanking plug
•   Push fit
9.   Secure fuel tank to vehicle chassis – see operation “Fuel Tank, Remove and Install”
10.   Connect 2x fuel line to fuel filter
Note:   Counterhold fuel filter at hexagon with open end of spanner.
•   Remove 4x blanking plug
11.   Connect 4x fuel lines (1)
•   Remove 4x blanking plug
•   Push fit



12.   Connect 2x fuel line to fuel rail
Note:   Counter hold adapter with open end of spanner.
•   Remove 4x blanking plug
