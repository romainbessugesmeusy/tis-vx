Camshaft Sensor, Motronic M1.5.6

 

Objective:

Checking operation of the camshaft sensor.

Measurement:

Plot of voltage at the signal output of the camshaft sensor.

Preparation:

Switch off the engine and ignition.

Connections:

INPUT A

COM/TRIGGER

INPUT B

Connect the red measurement lead to pin 2 of the plug connector for the camshaft sensor. Connect the black ground lead to ground.

 

 

Procedure Tech 31:

1. Download Reference curve.
2. Start engine and allow it to idle.
