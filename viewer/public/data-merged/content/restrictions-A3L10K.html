Restrictions

For the following systems and wiring harnesses, repair is not allowed:

  • Shielded lines/coaxial cables for anti-lock brake system/anti-skid control system, radio, telephone, motor, anti-theft warning system, for example. An exception to this rule are CAN-bus wires, they can be repaired as given in chapter Step D6 - Perform Repair
  • Airbag and pyrotechnic seat belt tensioner system
  • Electronically controlled throttle valve
  • Power steering system
  • Electrical seat adjustment
  • High-voltage live cables (e.g. ignition system, xenon lamp)

Note:   In single cases, within the framework of field remedies, deviations to these rules can be made.
