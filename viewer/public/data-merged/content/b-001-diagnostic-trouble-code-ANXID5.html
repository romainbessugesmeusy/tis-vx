P0110

Intake Air Temperature Circuit High Input

  • Throttle valve is closed for longer than 10 s (idle speed)
  • Intake air temperature is less than -36.75 °C (-32.80 °F)
    (Short circuit to voltage)

  • The control unit takes 20.25 °C (68.00 °F) as replacement value.

  • The learn functions are locked and current values are used for calculation.

9, 55 (X32)
