Introduction  

The Product  

The Speedster/VX 220 introduces a two-seater sports car to the model range. A major feature of the vehicle is the use of aluminium alloy and glassfibre composite materials in many structural parts of the vehicle.  

Active safety  

 

•  Tyre characteristics are specific to each axle.  

•  Independent suspension at the front and rear.  

•  Anti-lock brake system (ABS).  

•  Ventilated disc brakes at the front and rear.  

•  Third brake lamp.  

 

Passive safety  

 

•  Driver's airbag.  

•  Three-point seat belts with pyrotechnic tensioners for driver and passenger.  

•  Energy absorbing structures at the front, sides and rear of the vehicle.  

•  Roll bar.  

•  Fuel tank located within a crossmember at the middle of the vehicle.  

•  Fuel cutoff impact switch.  

•  Fire extinguisher is optionally available.  

 

  

 

Driving comfort  

 

•  Removable soft top or hardtop.  

•  Bucket-type seats.  

•  Driver's seat with longitudinal adjustment and lumbar support.  

•  Passenger's seat with longitudinal adjustment is optionally available.  

•  Shoulder pads for the seat belts are optionally available.  

•  Footrest for the passenger is optionally available.
